'''Marcia Wallace''' appears as a non-speaking background character in the episode "[[Super Best Friends]]" as [[George Bush|George Bush's]] maid, Maggie. She has not made another appearance.

==Appearance==
Marcia wears a green maid's gown, a white apron, and a white hat. She has curly red hair and wears red lipstick.

==Trivia==
*The character "Maggie" was conceived from a similar character of [[Trey Parker]] and [[Matt Stone|Matt Stone's]] sitcom, ''That's My Bush''.
