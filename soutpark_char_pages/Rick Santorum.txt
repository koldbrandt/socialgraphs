'''Rick Santorum''' is an American politician who appears in the [[Season Sixteen]] episode "[[Faith Hilling]]", wherein he appears at the [[Colorado Republican Debate (2012)|Colorado Republican Presidential Debate]] with [[Ron Paul]], [[Newt Gingrich]], and [[Mitt Romney]]. During the conclusion of the episode, Santorum, Gingrich, and Romney debate with the [[Talking Cat]], who replaces Paul.

==Appearance==
Rick Santorum can be seen wearing a black business suit consisting of black pants, black shoes, a black coat with a gray handkerchief in the pocket, and a gray shirt with a red tie. He has combed black hair.
