'''Mario Francesco Batali''' is an American celebrity chef featured in [[Season Fourteen]] episode "[[Crème Fraiche]]".

==Background==
Mario Batali was one of the several celebrity chefs who took part in a school cafeteria cooking competition against [[Randy Marsh]]. During the competition, Mario was seen flambéing some prawns.

==Appearance==
Mario Batali has light brown hair with a pigtail and short beard. He wears the standard chef's uniform with dark green socks and a pair of brown crocs. He also has a small watch on his left wrist.
