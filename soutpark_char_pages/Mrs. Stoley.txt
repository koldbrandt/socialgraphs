'''Mrs. Stoley''', formerly '''Mrs. Brook''', is the mother of [[Kevin Stoley]], who first appears in [[Season Six]]'s episode, "[[My Future Self n' Me]]". She and her [[Mr. Stoley|husband]] hire a "Future self", similar to Kevin. After that episode, it remained a mystery whether she and her husband were really Kevin's parents until [[Season Nineteen]]'s episode, "[[You're Not Yelping]]", where she is seen eating at [[Whistlin' Willy's]] with her husband and Kevin.

==Appearance==
Mrs. Stoley has short brown hair and brown eyebrows. She wears a light purple sweater, a black skirt and black shoes. She also wears gold earrings on both ears and is usually seen holding a black purse.
