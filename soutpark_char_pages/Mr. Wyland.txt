'''Mr. Wyland''' was the 3rd-grade substitute teacher for [[Herbert Garrison|Mr. Garrison]] in two episodes after Mr. Garrison was caught attempting to molest a child, [[Eric Cartman]], in "[[Cartman Joins NAMBLA]]". He was first shown in "[[Cherokee Hair Tampons]]", where he made the class make a get well card for [[Kyle Broflovski|Kyle]], who had kidney failure. He also appears in "[[Chef Goes Nanners]]" to have the children debate over the [[South Park Flag]].

==Appearance==
Wyland wears a blue jacket, similar to that of [[Randy Marsh]], with a red bow tie. He has a double chin, wrinkles on his forehead, and brown hair.

==Prominence==
*"[[Cherokee Hair Tampons]]" - His first appearance. He gets fooled by Kenny, Cartman, and Stan while taking attendance.
*"[[Chef Goes Nanners]]" - Assigns the flag debate to the class.
