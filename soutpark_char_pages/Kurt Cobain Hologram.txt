'''Kurt Cobain Hologram '''was featured in the [[Season Eighteen]] episode "[[HappyHolograms|#HappyHolograms]]". It is modeled after deceased musician Kurt Cobain.

==Background==
Kurt Cobain Hologram is a computer program designed by [[Syntech Hologram Company]]. It performed at the ''[[Washington Redskins: Go Fuck Yourself Holiday Special]]''. He is seen singing "Up on the Rooftop" dressed as an 18th-century chimney sweep while holding a gun, which is a reference to his suicide. [[CartmanBrah]] comments on him with the rest of the holiday special.

===Historical Information===
Kurt Cobain was the lead guitarist, vocalist, and founder of the alternative rock band Nirvana. 
He was born in Aberdeen, Washington on Feb 20, 1967, and was discovered dead on April 8, 1994. Kurt was one of the most well-known musicians of the "grunge" sub-genre in the early 1990s.

==Appearance==
Kurt has long blond hair and brown eyebrows. He wears a red Santa hat, a blue jacket, and a blue scarf
