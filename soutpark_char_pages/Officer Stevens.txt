'''Officer Stevens''' is a police officer in [[Park County Police Station]].

==Appearance==
Officer Stevens wears the standard police uniform. He appears with brown hair with thick sideburns.

==Prominence==
*"[[The China Probrem]]" - Appears as part of a standoff at [[P.F. Chang's]].
*"[[Pee]]" - Fed a monkey a banana during an experiment for a "vaccine" against urine exposure. Later in the experiment, as [[Randy Marsh]] was being peed on, the fire marshal ordered Stevens to prepare his gun. He is first identified by name here.
*"[[The Poor Kid]]" - Arrested [[Eric Cartman]] for filing a false police report.
*"[[HappyHolograms]]" - Tried to tell a joke of him jacking off to [[Lorde]] to the other police officers, which fails to make anyone laugh.
*"[[Naughty Ninjas]]" - Seen during a protest outside the police station and is harassed at [[Whole Foods Market]].
*"[[Splatty Tomato]]"
*"[[Dead Kids]]" - comforts Randy and lets him know he is a good husband.
*"[[The Pandemic Special]]" - Seen in the school with the rest of the police force as replacement teachers.

==''[[South Park: The Fractured But Whole]]''==
Officer Stevens appeared during [[The Thin White Line]] mission. Outside the police station, he was seen snorting cocaine with [[Officer Johnson]].

==Trivia==
*In three of his appearances, he interacts with [[Randy Marsh]] specifically, being ordered to shoot him, admitting to having jacked off to [[Lorde]] not aware it is Randy, and later telling him he is a good husband. 
*Despite the similar last name, he is not known to be related to [[Bebe Stevens]] or her family.
