'''Nellie Stotch''' is [[Butters Stotch|Butters]]' aunt. She lives in [[Los Angeles]], California with her husband, [[Budd Stotch|Budd]]. Her only appearances are in "[[AWESOM-O]]" and "[[Britney's New Look]]".

==Appearance==
She wears a pink, sleeveless button-up shirt, blue pants, and white shoes. She has wavy, blonde hair and small navy blue earrings.

==Personality==
She is very kind, being very loving to Butters in "[[AWESOM-O]]". She is pretty much the only family member who is entirely kind to Butters.

==Family==
===[[Budd Stotch]]===
She is married to [[Budd Stotch]], they have not been seen fighting, so it is assumed they have a fairly good marriage. She is oblivious to the fact that Budd molested Butters.

===[[Butters Stotch]]===
She is Butters' aunt, it is assumed she loves him very much, judging by her reaction when he first arrived from the airport in "[[AWESOM-O]]".

==Prominence==
*"[[AWESOM-O]]" - Greets Butters and [[Eric Cartman]], when they arrive from airport.
*"[[Britney's New Look]]" - Seen taking photos of [[Britney Spears]].
