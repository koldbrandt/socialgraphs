'''Matt Hasselbeck''' is a football player who played the position of quarterback for several National Football League teams throughout his career. He appears in the [[Season Fourteen]] episode, "[[Insheeption]]", as the football player entering [[Mr. Mackey]]'s dream. His role in the dream and episode is minimal and his speaking lines do not consist of more than a few words.

==Appearance==
Matt Hasselbeck wears his former Seattle Seahawks football uniform which consists of a blue jersey, a helmet, and pants with white trimming. He also wears white wrist sweat bands.
