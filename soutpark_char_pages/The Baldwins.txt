The '''Baldwin''' family of actors comprises [[Alec Baldwin|Alec]], Daniel, William (Billy), and Stephen. They appear in ''[[South Park: Bigger, Longer & Uncut]]''. [[Dave Foley]] provided the voices of all of the Baldwin brothers.<ref name="IMDb"/>

==Background==
The [[Canada|Canadian]] army bombs and kills the Baldwin family as well as the [[The Arquette Residence|Arquette]] family in retaliation for [[Terrance and Phillip]]'s imprisonment.

==Appearance==
The Baldwins all appeared in their underwear or wrapped in towels beside their pool before being bombed. Billy Baldwin had unusually shaped bright blue eyes.

==Personality==
They all appeared to be whiny, egotistical and self-centered. Even after all his brothers were killed, Stephen didn't seem to care, simply shouting, "Haha! You missed me!" before being bombed himself.
