The '''[[Teenagers|Teenager]] Boy with Blue Jacket''' was a character that appeared in some episodes of the first seasons. He usually appeared as a background character but had a few lines at a time while. His first appearance was in the [[Season Two]] episode "[[Clubhouses]]". 

==Appearance==
He has dirty blond hair with a military buzzcut and wears a white shirt under a turquoise jacket, black pants and black shoes. He has no other distinguishable characteristics.
