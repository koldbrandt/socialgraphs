'''Kevin George Knipfing''', better known as '''Kevin James''', is an American actor and comedian who appears in [[Season Fifteen]] episode "[[Funnybot]]".

==Background==
Kevin James was one of the several comedians who stormed into [[South Park Elementary]], armed with guns and demanding that the school put a stop to [[Funnybot (Character)|Funnybot]] who had left them out of work.

In "[[You're Getting Old]]", he was mentioned by [[Dr. Carroll]] when the latter attempted to show [[Stan Marsh]] an advertisement for ''The Zookeeper''.

==Appearance==
Kevin James has short brown hair and wears a red jacket over gray T-shirt, gray pants and black shoes.
