'''Nicolas Sarkozy''' was the 23rd [[wikipedia:President_of_France|President of France]]. He appears in "[[Pinewood Derby]]" as one of the world leaders that congratulated [[Randy Marsh]] for discovering warp drive spaceflight using the official Pinewood Derby race-car kit.

==Appearance==
Nicolas has black hair and a receding hairline. He wears a light blue business suit with a white shirt and a red tie. He has no other distinguishing features.
