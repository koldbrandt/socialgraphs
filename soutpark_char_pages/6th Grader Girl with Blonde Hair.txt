The '''6th Grader Girl with Blonde Hair''' is a student at [[South Park Elementary]] who usually appears as a background character. She is usually seen in the [[South Park Elementary Cafeteria|school cafeteria]] with other [[The 6th Graders|6th Graders]].

==Appearance==
[[File:Butterballs035.png|thumb|left|180px|Seen in Stan's anti-bullying video.]]
She has long blonde hair and wears a cyan coat over a red shirt and a dark blue skirt, sometimes she can be seen wearing a red coat.
