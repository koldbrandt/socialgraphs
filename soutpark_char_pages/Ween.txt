'''Ween''' was an American experimental rock band. They appear in the [[Season Two]] episode, "[[Chef Aid]]", playing at the concert to help [[Chef]] pay for some legal trouble.

==Background==
Ween performed the song "[[The Rainbow]]" on ''[[Chef Aid: The South Park Album]]''. They also appeared in the accompanying mockumentary, ''[[Chef Aid: Behind the Menu]]'', in which they talked about Chef's influence on their career.

==Trivia==
*Trey Parker and Matt Stone directed a music video for the song "Even if You Don't" from the ''White Pepper'' album.
*Trey and Matt's band [[DVDA]] was the opening band for Ween on several occasions during 1999 through 2000 era.
