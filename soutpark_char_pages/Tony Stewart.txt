'''Anthony Wayne Stewart''' is an American professional racing driver who appears in [[Season Fourteen]] episode, "[[Poor and Stupid]]".

==Background==
Several NASCAR drivers were interrupted by [[Eric Cartman]]'s off-topic and redneck-style comments during a NASCAR press conference. Tony Stewart was seen sitting to the left of Cartman, staring at the latter while he was speaking.

==Appearance==
Tony Stewart has short black hair and wears a red racing suit imprinted with the sponsoring company Office Depot.
