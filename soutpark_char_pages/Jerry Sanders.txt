'''Jerry Sanders''' is the former mayor of [[San Diego]]. He appears in the [[Season Sixteen]] episode, "[[Butterballs]]", where he was seen in a mock commercial advertising [[San Diego]]. In the commercial, a montage of various attractions and places to visit appear on a poorly implemented green-screen behind Jerry.

==Appearance==
Jerry Sanders wears a black business suit consisting of black shoes, black pants, a white shirt, a green patterned tie, and a black coat. He has short, receding gray hair and wrinkles on his face.
