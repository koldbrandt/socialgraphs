'''Josef "Sef" Furman''' was an 18-year-old ''South Park'' fan from Plano, Texas who suffered from brain cancer. With the help of the Make-A-Wish Foundation and the creators of the show, he made a guest appearance in "[[The Pandemic Special]]".<ref name="KRLD">{{cite web|url=https://www.radio.com/krld/entertainment/local/north-texas-teen-stars-in-south-park-episode|title=North Texas teen stars in 'South Park' episode|publisher=KRLD 1080}}</ref>

Sef passed away on November 26, 2020,<ref name="JF" /> almost two months after "[[The Pandemic Special]]" aired.

==Background==
Sef Furman appears in [[Mrs. Nelson]]'s 4th-grade Zoom class and is later seen in quarantine with the rest of the school.

==Appearance==
Sef Furman has black glasses and wears a black short-sleeved shirt, red shorts, and black shoes. In an interview, his father said that the creators designed the character to resemble Sef's real-life appearance.<ref name="KRLD" />

==Trivia==
*He is the fourth ''[[South Park]]'' fan to guest star on the show. The others were [[Alex Glick]] who appeared in "[[Red Man's Greed]]", [[Matthew Klinner]] who appeared at the end of the [[South Park Title Sequence|title sequence]] for "[[Sexual Healing]]", and [[Hugh]] who appeared in a few episodes of [[Season Twenty-Two]].
