'''Amethyst Amelia Kelly''', better known by her stage name '''Iggy Azalea''', is an Australian rapper, songwriter, and model. She first appear in the [[Season Eighteen]] episode, "[[REHASH|#REHASH]]".

==Background==
In her debut, she performed "[[Missy Who, Who]]" at the "Women of Rock" concert with the [[Michael Jackson Hologram]]. Before the performance, she got into a fight with [[Lorde]].

She returns in "[[HappyHolograms|#HappyHolograms]]", where she performs with [[Elvis Presley]]'s [[Elvis Presley Hologram|hologram]] in the ''[[Washington Redskins: Go Fuck Yourself Holiday Special]]''.

==Appearance==
Iggy Azalea has blonde hair and wears a light blue sleeveless jumpsuit, yellow high-heeled shoes, and much makeup. She also has immense, exaggerated, oversized buttocks.

==Prominence==
*"[[REHASH|#REHASH]]" - Fights [[Lorde]] at the "Women of Rock" concert.
*"[[HappyHolograms|#HappyHolograms]]" - Performs at the ''[[Washington Redskins: Go Fuck Yourself Holiday Special]]'' with a hologram of [[Elvis Presley Hologram|Elvis Presley]].
*"[[Safe Space]]" - Seen briefly on [[Eric Cartman|Cartman]]'s Twitter account in the section "''Who To Follow''".
