'''Emmett Hollis''' was a contestant in the Pinewood Derby competition in the eponymous [[Season Thirteen]] episode, "[[Pinewood Derby]]". He has the best car, but loses because [[Randy Marsh|Randy]] ends up cheating. His [[Mr. Hollis|father]], unable to bear the loss, commits suicide in front of him.

==Appearance==
He has light brown hair, with a prominent parting on the left. He wears the Pinewood Derby Scouts' uniform throughout the episode.
