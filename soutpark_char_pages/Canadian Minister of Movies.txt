The '''Canadian Minister of Streaming''' (formerly the '''Canadian Minister of Movies''') appears in ''[[South Park: Bigger, Longer & Uncut]]'', where he was seen debating [[Sheila Broflovski]] at the U.N. Building. He was then known as the Canadian Minister of Movies. He later appeared in "[[SUPER HARD PCness]]" as The Canadian Minister of Streaming.

==Appearance==
Like all Canadians he has a Pac-Man type head that flaps when he speaks. He wears a blue suit with a green shirt underneath, glasses and has black hair in the style of a comb-over. He also has a small mustache and a long beard.
