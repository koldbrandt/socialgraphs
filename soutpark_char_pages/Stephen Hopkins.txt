'''Stephen Hopkins''' appeared in the [[Season Seven]] episode, "[[I'm a Little Bit Country]]", where he was seen during [[Eric Cartman]]'s flashback. He was the Governor of the Colony of Rhode Island and Providence Plantations and was a member of the Continental Congress in 1776.

==Appearance==
He appears in a brown dress coat, brown trousers and red shirt. He had white hair and wears a red hat. Like many of the characters seen in Cartman's flashback, he was not drawn with normal ''[[South Park]]'' facial features, instead having realistic facial proportions.
