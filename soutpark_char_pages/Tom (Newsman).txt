'''Tom''' is a news anchor for [[Channel 4 News]]. He first appears in the [[Season Thirteen]] episode, "[[Pinewood Derby]]", reporting about an alien ship preparing to land. Since [[Season Twenty-Two]], he has been the primary news anchor.

==Appearance==
Tom has dark brown combed hair and wears a gray business suit consisting of a gray coat, a white collar shirt, a blue tie, gray pants, and black shoes.

==Prominence==
*"[[Pinewood Derby]]" - Makes a report outside about an alien ship's preparing to land.
*"[[Coon 2: Hindsight]]" - reports on the devastation at the [[Gulf of Mexico]]
*"[[1%]]" - black-haired version of him reports on [[Butters Stotch]] and the Occupy movement.
*"[[Sponsored Content]]" - Appears with the [[newsmen]].
*"[[Truth and Advertising]]" - Appears with the newsmen.
*"[[SUPER HARD PCness]]" - interviews [[Kyle Broflovski]] and the [[Canadian Minister of Streaming]] as anchor.
*"[[Mexican Joker]]" - reports on [[Alejandro White|Mexican Joker]]
*"[[The Pandemic Special]]" - reports on the [[COVID-19]] pandemic
*"[[South ParQ Vaccination Special]]" - reports on the [[COVID-19 Vaccine]] and argues with [[Bill Keegan]] about it.
