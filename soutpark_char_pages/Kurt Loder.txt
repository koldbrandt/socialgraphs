'''Kurt Loder''' appears in "[[Timmy 2000]]", as the host of ''MTV News''. In "[[Scott Tenorman Must Die]]", Kurt is seen interviewing [[Radiohead]].

==Appearance==
Kurt has abnormally large ears, as well as various facial wrinkles. He wears a black coat, black pants, and a black undershirt.
