The '''6th Grader with Green Jacket''' is a student at [[South Park Elementary]]. He is a background character and is usually seen with other [[The 6th Graders|6th graders]] in the [[South Park Elementary Cafeteria|cafeteria]].

==Appearance==
He is a short 6th Grader with spiky brown hair and wears a green jacket over a gray shirt and dark brown pants.
