'''Tom Shane''' is the president and spokesperson for The Shane Company,<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.bizjournals.com/atlanta/stories/2002/02/18/smallb3.html?page=all|title=Atlanta Business Chronicle on Tom Shane|publisher=bizjournals.com}}</ref> a fine jewelry retailer in Colorado. Shane appears in the [[Season Eleven]] episode "[[Guitar Queer-O]]", where he was seen as a local celebrity at [[Charles Kincade]]'s party.

Other famous people in attendance include [[Jay Cutler]], formerly of the [[Denver Broncos]], and [[Jake Jabs]] of American Furniture Warehouse, another local Colorado company.

==Appearance==
He wears a blue tailcoat and has combed-down brown hair.
