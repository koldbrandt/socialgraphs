'''John and Patricia Ramsey''' appeared in "[[Butters' Very Own Episode]]" in which they join [[Butters Stotch|Butters]]' parents in searching for [[Some Puerto Rican Guy]], who allegedly kidnapped or killed their respective children.

==Appearance==
===John===
John has brown, graying hair, as well as several facial wrinkles. He wears a blue suit, white shirt, and striped, green tie.

===Patricia===
Patricia had brown hair. She wore a white-trimmed, black coat, white undershirt, and black pants.
