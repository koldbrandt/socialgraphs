'''Wild Dogs''' are wild animals featured in ''[[South Park: The Stick of Truth]]''. They are usually found in the [[Lost Forest]]. 

==Appearance==
The wild dogs have either brown or grey fur.

==Abilities==
*'''Call of the Wild''' - When only one dog is remaining, that dog will summon another ally to join the battle.
*'''Bad Mangy Mutt''' - Inflicts damage and the Grossed Out status effect if not blocked.
*'''Bad Ferocious Mutt''' - Inflicts damage and the Screwed status effect if not blocked.
