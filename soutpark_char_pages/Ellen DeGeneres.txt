'''Ellen DeGeneres''' is an American actress, comedian, and TV talk show host. She appears in the [[Season Thirteen]] episode "[[Fishsticks]]" where she had [[Eric Cartman]] and [[Jimmy Valmer]] on her talk show, ''[[The Ellen DeGeneres Show]]''. She also appears as one of the comedians in "[[Funnybot]]".

==Appearance==
Ellen has blonde hair. She wears a gray jacket, a white shirt, and blue jeans. She also appears to have a very faint mustache.
