The '''New York Boy with Red Outfit''' is a character who appears in the [[Season Three]] episode, "[[World Wide Recorder Concert]]", where he is seen as one of the friends of the [[New York Kids]] [[Smitty|leader]]. He is also one of several cut characters from ''[[South Park: The Stick of Truth]]'' and he is seen on a poster at [[Raisins (Location)|Raisins]] in ''[[South Park: The Fractured But Whole]]''.

==Appearance==
He has combed Black hair and wears a White shirt under a bright Red outfit with White stripes on the sides and a Gold chain. He has Black skin in the episode, but in his game appearance, he has Tanned skin.

==Prominence==
*"[[World Wide Recorder Concert]]" - He and the other [[New York Kids]] antagonize the boys during the episode.
*"[[Raisins]]" - Seen on a poster at Raisins.
*"[[You Got F'd in the A]]" - Seen on a poster at [[Raisins (Location)|Raisins]].

==Trivia==
*Like Smitty, he also appears in the [[Raisins (Location)|Raisins Restaurant]] on a poster.
*In the ''[[South Park: The Stick of Truth]]'' game files, his character model is described as "newyork_2".
