'''Chad Handler''' is [[List of Male 4th Graders|male fourth grader]] who appears in several episodes with other handicapped children.

==Background==
Chad made his first appearance in "[[Up the Down Steroid]] " at the Special Olympics, later he appears in "[[Crippled Summer]] " on [[Lake Tardicaca]] , he is on the [[Red Team]] along with [[Nathan]], [[Mimsy]], [[Francis (Special Ed)|Francis]], [[Michael (Special Ed)|Michael]], [[Dennis Murray]], and [[Reggie Burke]].

==Appearance==
Chad is usually seen with a pink bicycle helmet and a blue backpack. He has brown hair and is almost always baring his teeth,his head is usually tilted to the right. Chad's current outfit is probably a plain red long-sleeved shirt, yellow pants, and black shoes. In "[[Crippled Summer]]" he wore a shirt similar, but with the Lake Tardicaca logo on the right. In "[[Sponsored Content]]" Chad wore a light blue ill-fitting sweater with a red tie and black pants. In "[[Moss Piglets]]" he wore his current outfit under a white coat.
