'''Mrs. Harris''' is [[Billy Harris]]' mother. She appears in [[Season Four]] episode "[[Trapper Keeper]]", where she brought her son to school for his class elections so that he could cast his vote for class president.

==Appearance==
She has long brown hair and wears a navy blue jumper and a dark gray skirt.
