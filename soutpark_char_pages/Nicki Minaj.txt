'''Onika Tanya Maraj-Petty''', better known professionally as '''Nicki Minaj''', is a rapper and singer known for her songs such as "Tusa" and "Anaconda". She appears in the [[Season Eighteen]] episode, "[[REHASH|#REHASH]]".

==Background==
Nicki was one of the singers performing for the "Women of Rock" concert held in [[Pepsi Center]], alongside [[Lorde]], [[Miley Cyrus]], and [[Iggy Azalea]].

==Appearance==
Nicki Minaj wears a colorful cocktail jumpsuit with a glitter bling skirt. She also wears high-heel boots and visibly has a lot of make-up.
