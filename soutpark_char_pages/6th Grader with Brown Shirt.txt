The '''6th Grader with brown shirt''' is a student at [[South Park Elementary]] who only appears as a background character. He is usually seen in the [[South Park Elementary Cafeteria|cafeteria]] talking to other [[The 6th Graders|6th Graders]].

==Appearance==
He is a medium-sized 6th grader with short brown hair and wears a brown shirt with a beige collar and gray pants.
