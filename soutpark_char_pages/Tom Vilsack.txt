'''Tom Vilsack''' is the United States Secretary of Agriculture and head of the [[USDA]]. He appears in the [[Season Eighteen]] episode, "[[Gluten Free Ebola]]", in which he leads the effort to find a solution to the anti-gluten panic in the United States, spurred on by [[South Park (Location)|South Park]].

==Appearance==
Tom Vilsack wears a black business suit, which consists of black shoes, black pants, a black coat, a white shirt, and a blue tie, as well as a white lab coat and a USDA name badge. He has combed blond hair.
