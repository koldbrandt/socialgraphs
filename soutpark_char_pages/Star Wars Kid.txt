'''Ghyslain Raza''', popularly known as the '''Star Wars Kid''', is a Canadian internet celebrity who appears in the [[Season Twelve]] episode, "[[Canada on Strike]]".

==Background==
Star Wars kid is one of the internet celebrities waiting for their theoretical payments. He is shot by [[Tay Zonday]] during the brawl between the other internet celebrities.

==Appearance==
Star Wars Kid wears brown shoes, khaki pants, and a blue striped shirt. He has brown hair, which has a "bowl-cut" style. He can be seen wielding a plain retriever golf club.

Unlike other Canadians, the Star Wars Kid does not have the Pac-Man type head and beady eyes typically seen.
