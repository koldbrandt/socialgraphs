'''Margaret Nelson''' was a [[The 4th Grade|fourth-grade]] teacher at [[South Park Elementary]] after [[Herbert Garrison]] left to run for President. She first appeared in the [[Season Nineteen]] episode, "[[The City Part of Town]]" and died from [[COVID-19]] in the [[South ParQ Vaccination Special|Vaccination Special]].

==Appearance==
Margaret wore a skirt, usually blue or black, a magenta v-neck shirt, and a white undershirt. She also wore black, flat shoes and a small wristwatch. She had black hair, which she styles in a bun, and black eyelashes. She had the stereotypical characteristics of Asians seen in the series such as small eyes.

==Prominence==
*"[[The City Part of Town]]" - First speaking role as the Fourth Grade teacher.
*"[[Truth and Advertising]]" - Second speaking role.
*"[[Skank Hunt]]" - Seen speaking with police officers in the school hallway.
*"[[Douche and a Danish]]" - Seen trying to teach the class when [[Herbert Garrison|Mr. Garrison]] barges in and tries to teach.
*"[[Hummels & Heroin]]" - Oversees [[Marcus Preston]]'s presentation in the [[South Park Elementary|school]] cafeteria.
*"[[SUPER HARD PCness]]" - Attends school staff meetings.
*"[[Dead Kids]]" - Hands over math exams to the students and explains them again the addition of fractions and later seen being interviewed by police about one of the school shootings.
*"[[A Boy And A Priest]]" - Seen in church.
*"[[The Problem with a Poo]]" - Watches [[Mr. Hankey]] leave [[South Park (Location)|South Park]].
*"[[Buddha Box]]" - Pulls the Buddha Box off [[Eric Cartman]]'s head.
*"[[Bike Parade]]" - Seen watching the bike parade.
*"[[Shots|Shots!!!]]" - Seen at the PTA meeting.
*"[[Let Them Eat Goo]]" - Ate a [[Tegridy Burger]] in the middle of the class.
*"[[Tegridy Farms Halloween Special]]" - Seen at the school assembly.
*"[[Turd Burglars]]" - Seen at the women's meeting at [[Park County Community Center]].
*"[[The Pandemic Special]]" - Taught the class via online meetings.
*"[[South ParQ Vaccination Special]]" - She dies from COVID-19 before getting the [[COVID-19 Vaccine|vaccine]]. Her first name is revealed as "Margaret" at her funeral.

==Trivia==
*Mrs. Nelson was the third teacher to teach the same [[The 4th Grade|4th Grade]] class, with the first being [[Diane Choksondik|Ms. Choksondik]] and the second, [[Herbert Garrison|Mr. Garrison]].
