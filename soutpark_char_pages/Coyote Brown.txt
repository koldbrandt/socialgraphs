'''Coyote Brown''' is a stand-up comedian who appears in the [[Season Eleven]] episode, "[[With Apologies to Jesse Jackson]]".

==Background==
While performing a stand-up comedy in the [[Laugh Factory]], Coyote Brown sees [[Randy Marsh]] in the audience and points him out, referencing the latter's incident in the ''[[Wheel of Fortune]]'' show, and addressing him as the "n****r guy".

==Appearance==
Coyote Brown has black buzz-cut hair and horseshoe mustache. He wears a black jacket over a white shirt and a silver pendant, black pants, and white sneakers.
