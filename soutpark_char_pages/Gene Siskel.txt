'''Gene Siskel''' was seen as a denizen of [[Hell]]. He appeared once in "[[Mr. Hankey's Christmas Classics]]" and again in "[[Do the Handicapped Go to Hell?]]".

==Background==
Gene Siskel spent most of his time on Earth watching, writing and critiquing movies. He died on February 20, 1999.

==Appearance==
He was animated using a cut-out of his real face and a drawn cartoon body.

==Prominence==
*"[[Mr. Hankey's Christmas Classics]]" - Kissing [[Diana, Princess of Wales]].
*"[[Do the Handicapped Go to Hell?]]" A party guest at the luau.
