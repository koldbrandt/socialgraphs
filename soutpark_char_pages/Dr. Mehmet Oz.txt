'''Doctor Oz''' is an American talk show host and a cardiac surgeon. He appears in the [[Season Sixteen]] episode "[[Butterballs]]", where he was seen interviewing [[Stan Marsh]] and [[Butters Stotch]] about "[[Make Bullying Kill Itself]]". After making Butters angry by forcing him to speak out against bullying, he is attacked and beaten up by Butters on his television show.

==Appearance==
Doctor Oz wears blue medical scrubs consisting of blue pants and a blue shirt. He has wavy black hair and wears black shoes.
