'''Pope Francis''', born '''Jorge Mario Bergoglio''', is the current leader of the Catholic Church who appears in the [[Season Seventeen]] episode, "[[The Hobbit]]". Following the resignation of [[Pope Benedict XVI]] in 2013, Pope Francis became promoted as the head of the Vatican church.

==Background==
In "[[The Hobbit]]", Pope Francis appeared to receive the honor of ''Time'' magazine's "Person of the Year" for 2013. Upon receiving the award, he is interrupted by [[Kanye West]], who tries to convince the audience that [[Kardashians|Kim Kardashian]] is not a hobbit. As the Pope tries to correct him, Kanye pushes him aside.

==Appearance==
Pope Francis has thinning, gray hair and a wrinkled face. He wears modest, gray, and white papal garments, a silver crucifix around his neck, and a white zucchetto.
