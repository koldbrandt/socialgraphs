The '''Alien Guard''' is a [[Visitors|Visitor]] fought in ''[[South Park: The Stick of Truth]]''.

==Background==
In [[Alien Abduction]] quest, alien guards are scattered aboard the alien spaceship which abducted [[The New Kid]]. They patrol the spaceship and fight the New Kid whenever they encounter him.

==Appearance==
The guards wear police hats and a uniform. They also carry a space taser.

==Abilities==
*'''Melee Attack''' - An basic attack that hits three times.
*'''Perfect Dodge Stance''' - For a couple turns, the Alien Guard cannot be hit by normal attacks and can only be damaged by abilities.
*'''Space Taser''' - A single hitting attack that inflicts Attack Down if not blocked.
