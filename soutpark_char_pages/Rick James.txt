'''Rick James''' is an American musician. He appears in the [[Season Two]] episode "[[Chef Aid]]", where he gives [[The Boys|the boys]] money to help out [[Chef]]. Rick James also appears on ''[[Chef Aid: The South Park Album]]'', singing "[[Love Gravy]]" with [[Chef Aid: Behind the Menu#Ike Turner|Ike Turner]].

==Appearance==
He has long, black, "cornrowed" hair. He wears a black, long-sleeve shirt, and a gold, circular earring on his left ear.

==Personality==
He was shown to be kind and caring, giving [[The Boys|the boys]] money to help [[Chef]] in "[[Chef Aid]]".
