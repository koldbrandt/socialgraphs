The '''Handicapped Boy with Blue Jumpsuit''' is a unnamed handicapped boy who appears in several episodes with other handicapped children. He made his first appearance on "[[Up the Down Steroid]]" where he is seen training for the special Olympics. He also appears in "[[Crippled Summer]]" where he is seen on Lake Tardicaca as a member of the [[Red Team]]. So far he has played no major role.

==Appearance==
He has a tuft of messy blond hair and is usually seen with a smiling face. He is usually seen with his eyes closed. He wears a cyan blue shirt under a dark blue jumpsuit with two front pockets.

In "[[Crippled Summer]]" he wore the standard red uniform of Tardicaca Camp, and in "[[Moss Piglets]]" he wore a lab coat with a badge on the right side.
