'''George Burns''', born Nathan Birnbaum, was a famous comedian and actor. Burns appeared in the episode, "[[Cancelled]]", as one of the forms [[Najix]] took to appear more familiar to the boys. He also appeared as a resident of Hell in "[[Do the Handicapped Go to Hell?]]" and "[[Hell on Earth 2006]]".

[[Kenny McCormick|Kenny]] sees George Burns as he falls into Hell in ''[[South Park: Bigger, Longer & Uncut]]''.

==Appearance==
George is depicted as an elderly man wearing a black tuxedo, and round coke-bottle glasses. He is usually seen holding a long, vintage cigar.
