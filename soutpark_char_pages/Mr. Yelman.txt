'''Mr. Yelman''' appeared in the [[Season Fourteen]] episode, "[[Insheeption]]". Throughout the episode, he was believed to be a hoarder but in reality, was a sheep herder. After the group finds out [[Mr. Mackey]]'s hoarding was caused by [[Woodsy Owl]], Woodsy becomes angered and kills Mr. Yelman. After everyone awoke, Mr. Yelman was seen decapitated in his chair.

==Appearance==
Mr. Yelman is a sheep herder. He wears a bandanna on his head with a black strap. He wears a beige robe with a teal border. He also wears a long brown sweater. He can be seen holding a sheep herding staff.
