The '''South Park Sounderoos''' are a local country music duo by musicians Earl and Kate. They appear in the [[Season Twenty-Three]] episode, "[[Band in China]]".

==Background==
They perform a song at the [[Autumn Fest]], with [[Mayor McDaniels]] announcing that [[Stan Marsh]]'s band, [[Crimson Dawn]] will be performing next.
