'''Tupac Shakur''', known by his stage name '''2Pac''', was an American rapper. He appeared in the [[Season Eighteen]] episode, "[[REHASH|#REHASH]]", as a hologram created by [[Syntech Hologram Company]], ordered to capture the escaped [[Michael Jackson]] hologram. He also sleeps with [[Sharon Marsh]]. In the episode, "[[HappyHolograms|#HappyHolograms]]", he walks into the [[Park County Police Station]], teams up with Michael Jackson, and helps him kill the [[Record Producer]].

==Appearance==
Tupac Shakur appears as a hologram of the real Tupac Shakur. He is bald with a black goatee and mustache, and he wears brown shoes, a silver cross-chain, blue jeans and a belt, and visible white underwear. He also has many tattoos on his body and arms.
