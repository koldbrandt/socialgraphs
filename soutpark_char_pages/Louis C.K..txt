'''Louis Székely''', better known by his stage name '''Louis C.K.''', is an American stand-up comedian that appears in [[Season Fifteen]] episode "[[Funnybot]]".

==Background==
Louis C.K. was one of several comedians who witnessed the burial of [[Tyler Perry]] towards the end of the episode.

==Appearance==
Louis C.K. has balding brown hair and beard. He wears a black T-shirt, blue pants and black shoes.
