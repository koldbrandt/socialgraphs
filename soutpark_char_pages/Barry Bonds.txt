'''Barry Bonds '''is a former American baseball player who played for the Pittsburgh Pirates and San Francisco Giants. He appears in the episode "[[Up the Down Steroid]]" in which he presented the award at the Special Olympics, along with [[Jason Giambi]] and [[Mark McGwire]]. All three athletes have been linked or allegedly linked to the use of steroids. He reappears again in "[[200]]" and "[[201]]".

==Personality==
Although he was not given much screen time, the fact that he appeared to present the awards at the Special Olympics must mean he is somewhat kind.

==Appearance==
He appears to have a very large neck, as well as small facial features. He wears his white San Francisco Giants baseball uniform with "BONDS" at its center. He also appears to wear a black and white belt.

*"[[200]]" - Seen with the other celebrities suing South Park.
