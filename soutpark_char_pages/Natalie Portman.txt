'''Natalie Portman''' is an Israeli-American actress. In the episode "[[A History Channel Thanksgiving]]", she crashes into Pilgrim leader, [[Miles Standish]] with her truck after he lands on Earth.

==Appearance==
Natalie is short in stature. She wears a black navy-style jacket, grey pants, and pink lipstick. She has long brown hair and a small mole on the left side of her face.

==Personality==
She seems coy and shy about showing her 'wormhole' to anyone and she apparently knows about stuffing.

==Powers and Abilities==
Natalie is the portal between the worlds Earth and [[Plymouth (Planet)|Plymouth]], called the wormhole.

==Relationships==
She was taken on a date by [[Kyle Broflovski|Kyle of DeVry Institute]] to an expensive restaurant in order to persuade her to open her 'wormhole' per orders of the History Channel.

*'''Natalie Portman:''' "Hmmmm... no!" from "[[A History Channel Thanksgiving]]"

==Trivia==
*Natalie Portman played an identical role in the movie ''Thor'' released in the summer of 2011.
*Natalie Portman claims to be taking courses at "The University of Jerusalem". In reality, the name of her college institution is "Hebrew University".
*Natalie's surname was used as a pun for Transport Man, for her ability in transporting Miles Standish back to his home planet.
*In "[[The End of Serialization as We Know It]]", [[Gerald]]'s Internet search history includes having sent a tweet to her.
