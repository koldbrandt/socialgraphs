'''Lynell Griffin''', better known as '''Bubb Rubb''', appears in [[Season Twelve]] episode "[[Canada on Strike]]", as one of the internet celebrities waiting for their theoretical payments. He may or may not be deceased because he was not seen fighting or dead.

==Appearance==
He has short, black hair, and wears an Oakland Raiders knit beanie. He also appears to be slightly obese. He wears a black football jersey with number 53 (Rod Martin) on it, a brown coat, light brown pants, and a white earring on his right ear.
