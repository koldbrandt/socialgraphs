The '''[[Teenagers|Teenager]] Girl with Violet Shirt''' is a [[South Park (Location)|South Park]] [[City People|citizen]] who first appears in the [[Season Two]] episode, "[[Clubhouses]]". Like most teenagers from the early seasons, she hasn't made a recent appearance.

==Appearance==
She has short light brown hair, thin brown eyebrows and black eyelashes. She wears a short-sleeved violet shirt with a round neckline, blue jeans and black shoes. In "[[Clubhouses]]" she had "garbage" written on her shirt.
