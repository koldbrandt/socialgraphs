The''' [[Teenagers|Teenager]] Boy with [[Scuzzlebutt]] Shirt''' is a character that used to appear in the background of previous seasons, usually seen with other teens. He made his first appearance in the [[Season Four]] episode "[[Timmy 2000]]". He can be seen speaking briefly on the episode "[[Butters' Bottom Bitch]]". He is currently one of the PC brothers of [[PC Delta]].

==Appearance==
He wears a dark cyan shirt with a picture of the [[Scuzzlebutt]] head in a purple color. He has brown hair and freckles. In "[[Butters' Bottom Bitch]]" he wore a dark blue shirt with "ATO" on the right, a white shirt underneath and brown pants.
