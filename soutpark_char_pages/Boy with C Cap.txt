The '''Boy with C Cap''' is a background character in ''[[South Park]]''. He occasionally appears in the background throughout the show, mostly in large crowds, at parties, field trips, or in the [[South Park Elementary|school]] hallways. He appears in the background of "[[Tweek vs. Craig]]", watching the fight between [[Tweek Tweak]] and [[Craig Tucker]]. In "[[Funnybot]]" he is seen sleeping in [[Jimmy Valmer]]'s comedy awards.

His name remains unknown and he has yet to have any speaking roles.

==Appearance==
He wears gray-brown pants, black shoes, a blue button-down shirt with a collar, red gloves, brown hair and a red baseball cap with a black brim and the letter C on it. Sometimes in his cameos, the C on his cap is missing.
