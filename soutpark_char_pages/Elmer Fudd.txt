'''Elmer Fudd''' is a character who appears in the [[Season Fourteen]] episode, "[[Crippled Summer]]".

==Background==
He is a student at [[Lake Tardicaca|Tardicaca Camp]], which is a camp for children with disabilities. He was on the [[Blue Team]] along with [[Jimmy]], [[Timmy]], [[Lenora]], and [[Droopy Dawg]]. His disability makes him look and sound like Elmer Fudd from Merrie Melodies.

==Appearance==
He wears a blue cap with a gray border, a blue hood with the Camp Tardicaca logo, and light blue pants. He has a large nose and has a large and rather unusual head than other children. He seems to be bald.
