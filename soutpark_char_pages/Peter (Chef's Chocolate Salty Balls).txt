'''Peter''', also known as '''Roger''', '''Roman''' or '''Kevin''' is a [[South Park (Location)|South Park]] [[City People|citizen]] who first appears in the [[Season Two]] episode, "[[Chef's Chocolate Salty Balls]]". 

==Background==
Peter makes his first debut in "[[Chef's Chocolate Salty Balls]]" where he is seen in the [[Bijou Cinema]]. In "[[It Hits the Fan]]", he is seen with the crowd who repeatedly say "shit" after the word is said on national television. He previously worked as [[Maury Povich]]'s Stagehand on "[[Freak Strike]]" and currently works as a cameraman and replaces the [[cameraman|blond-haired cameraman]] in the recent Seasons. 

He also appears in ''[[South Park: The Fractured But Whole]]'', where he can be found near the bus stop where he can be selfied with. For undisclosed reasons, Peters' name was changed to "Roman".

==Appearance==
He has curly dark brown hair and wears a white shirt under a red hood, beige pants and black shoes. As a cameraman he wears a long-sleeved blue shirt under a gray waistcoat and wears a dark green back-facing cap. In ''[[South Park: The Fractured But Whole]]'' he wore a green hood instead of a red one.
