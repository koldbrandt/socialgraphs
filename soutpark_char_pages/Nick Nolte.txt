'''Nick Nolte''' is an actor who was a presenter at the 42nd Annual Nobel Prize Awards in the [[Season Three]] episode "[[Spontaneous Combustion]]". 

==Background==
During the award ceremony, Nick Nolte reads the nominees for Nobel Prize in Science who were listed as follows:
*Herald R. Pinkerton, for his further development of the grand unified theory of physics.
*[[Randy Marsh]], who won, for his formulated break-wind theory of spontaneous combustion.
*[[Dr. Alphonse Mephesto]] for his seven-assed Galapagos turtle.

==Appearance==
Nick Nolte has blond hair and is wearing a tuxedo with a white shirt and a black bow tie. He has wrinkles on his face and neck, he has visible signs of aging, and his hair appears to have been dyed.
