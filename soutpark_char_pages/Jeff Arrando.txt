'''Jeff Arrando''', also referred to as the '''Hispanic Man with Gravy Stains on his Lapel''' by his fellow news reporter, is a [[Channel 4 News]] reporter who appears in "[[The Entity]]", where he announced various [[celebrities]] arriving for [[Herbert Garrison|Mr. Garrison]]'s seminar. During the news report, he continually brushed the gravy on his lapel.

==Appearance==
Jeff Arrando, true to his nickname, is a Hispanic man with gravy stains on his lapel. He wore a mud brown jacket with a white shirt and tie underneath. He had black hair and a black mustache.
