The '''Tall Handicapped Boy with Blond Hair''' is a student at [[South Park Elementary]] who appears in several episodes with other disabled children. He made his first appearance on the [[Season Eight]] episode "[[Up the Down Steroid]]".

==Appearance==
He is a tall boy with short blond curly hair, blond eyebrows, big ears and is usually seen with a smile in his mouth. He wears an orange short-sleeved shirt, purple pants (pale blue in "[[Up the Down Steroid]]") over the belly button and black shoes.

In "[[Crippled Summer]]" he was seen wearing the red Tardicaca uniform and in one scene he was wearing a green summer shirt, purple shorts and blue flip flops and in "[[Moss Piglets]]" he was wearing a lab coat over his shirt and safety goggles for the science fair.
