'''Grandma Tucker''' is the grandmother of [[Tricia Tucker|Tricia]] and [[Craig Tucker]]. She was first mentioned in "[[Pandemic]]" and later pictured in "[[Pandemic 2: The Startling]]".

==Appearance==
Grandma Tucker has long gray hair as well as various facial wrinkles. She wears a light purple sweater, white shirt, and light brown pants.

==Prominence==
*"[[Pandemic]]" - Mentioned to have Craig $100 for his birthday.
*"[[Pandemic 2: The Startling]]" - Pictured.

== Trivia ==
*Technically speaking, it is because of her that Craig was asked to join the Peruvian flute band with Eric, Stan, Kyle, and Kenny, since it was her that gave Craig $100, so it was basically thanks to her that the ancient prophecy was fulfilled.
