}}'''Boris Johnson''' is a British politician who is currently serving as the Prime Minister of the [[United Kingdom]] and was formerly the Mayor of London. He is seen in the [[Season Twenty]] episode, "[[Members Only]]".

==Background==
In "[[Members Only]]", President [[Herbert Garrison|Garrison]] arrives at the [[Pentagon]] and is given various tools and devices. When he arrives at the Diplomatic Strategy and Negotiating room, Johnson gives him a call warning him not to eat [[Memberberries]]. Garrison, however, is not receptive and aggressively disregards his advice.

==Appearance==
Boris Johnson wears a blue formal suit, a white shirt, and a purple tie. He has light blond hair which is styled messily. He also has blond eyebrows.
