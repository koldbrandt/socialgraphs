'''Libby "Lenora" Perkins''' is a [[List of Female 4th Graders|female fourth-grader]] at [[South Park Elementary]]. She first appears in the [[Season Eight]] episode, "[[Up the Down Steroid]]".

==Background==
She is seen training for the Special Olympics in the episode "[[Up the Down Steroid]]", but appears prominently in the [[Season Fourteen]] episode, "[[Crippled Summer]]". She is in the Blue Team along with [[Jimmy]] and [[Timmy]], she also appears at the end of the episode singing at the talent show behind Jimmy. She was called "Lenora" by the [[Steve|Counselor Steve]] in this episode.

Libby appears in ''[[South Park: The Fractured But Whole]]'' DLC [[Bring the Crunch]] and can take a selfie with the player. She was renamed "Libby Perkins" in the game and her voice is also different.

==Appearance==
Libby has blond hair with braids, thick, spiky eyebrows and a slack mouth. She wears a pale pink shirt with a flower design in the middle, blue pants and black shoes. She wore pink gloves in ''[[South Park: The Fractured But Whole]]''.

In "[[Crippled Summer]]" she wears the standard uniform of Lake Tardicaca.

In "[[Sponsored Content]]" Libby wears a green dress with a black belt.
