'''Hakeem Korashki''' is an Iraqi police officer. He arrested [[Ms. Ellen]] in the [[Season One]] episode, "[[Tom's Rhinoplasty]]".

==Background==
Hakeem Korashki, and his team of Iraqi officers, arrest substitute teacher Ms. Ellen. They claim that she is a traitor to Iraq and has killed thousands. As proof, Hakeem shows a black-and-white photo of Ms. Ellen, with [[Saddam Hussein]], to [[Principal Victoria]].
