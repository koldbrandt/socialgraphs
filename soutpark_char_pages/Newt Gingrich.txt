'''Newt Gingrich''' is a [[United States]] politician who served as the U.S. Representative for Georgia's 6th congressional district. He appears in the [[Season Sixteen]] episode, "[[Faith Hilling]]", where he was seen at the [[Colorado Republican Debate (2012)|Colorado Republican Debate]] debating with [[Ron Paul]], [[Mitt Romney]], and [[Rick Santorum]].

He was briefly seen in "[[The Damned]]" introducing [[Herbert Garrison|Mr. Garrison]] at his presidential rally.

==Appearance==
Newt Gingrich wears a black business suit consisting of black shoes, black pants, a black coat, a white shirt, and a blue tie with purple stripes. He has thick white hair and is noticeably overweight.

==Prominence==
*"[[Faith Hilling]]" - Seen at the debate.
*"[[The Damned]]" - Seen introducing [[Herbert Garrison|Mr. Garrison]] at his rally.
