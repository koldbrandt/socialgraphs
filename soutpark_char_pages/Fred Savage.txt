'''Fred Savage''' is an American actor who appears in the [[Season Two]] episode "[[Chef's Chocolate Salty Balls]]", as one of the actors suggested to appear in the movie based on [[Mr. Hankey]]. He reappears in the [[Season Fourteen]] episode "[[200]]" along with the other outraged celebrities.

==Appearance==
Fred Savage wears black pants and a white shirt with "I'm Fred Savage!" written in red. He has black hair.
