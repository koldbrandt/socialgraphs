The '''Handicapped Girl with Brown Hair''' is a student at [[South Park Elementary]] who appears as a background character in some episodes. She is part of the [[Blue Team]], along with [[Jimmy Valmer|Jimmy]] and [[Timmy Burch|Timmy]].

==Appearance==
She has messy light brown hair and is always seen with her eyes slightly closed. Her head seems to be tilted down.

==Prominence==
*"[[Up the Down Steroid]]" - Seen training for the Special Olympics.
*"[[Crippled Summer]]" - Appears in [[Lake Tardicaca]] with the other handicapped children.
*"[[Black Friday]]" - Part of [[Stan Marsh|Stan]]'s army to get the PlayStation 4.
*"[[Titties and Dragons]]"
*"[[Handicar]]"
*"[[Moss Piglets]]" - Seen as a participant at the science fair.
