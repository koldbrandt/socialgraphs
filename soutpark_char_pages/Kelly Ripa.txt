'''Kelly Maria Ripa''' is an American actress and talk show host who appears in the [[Season Thirteen]] episode, "[[Eat, Pray, Queef]]".

==Background==
The [[Katherine and Katie Queef|Queef Sisters]] are invited to a talk show hosted by Kelly Ripa and [[Regis Philbin]]. During the interview, the Queef Sisters present their [[Eat, Pray, Queef (Book)|new book]], queefing several times as well. Kelly Ripa applauds them as the driving force for the feminist movement, before raising her legs and letting a queef out herself, much to the disgust of Regis Philbin.

==Appearance==
Kelly Ripa has a long, blonde hair, and wears a turquoise blouse, gray skirt with belt, and brown shoes.
