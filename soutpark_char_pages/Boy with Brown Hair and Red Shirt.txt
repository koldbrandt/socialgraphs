The '''Boy with Brown Hair and Red Shirt''' is a student in [[South Park Elementary]] who first appeared in the [[Season Eight]] episode, "[[Quest for Ratings]]".

==Appearance==
The Boy with Brown Hair and Red Shirt has brown hair combed to the sides and wore black shoes, gray pants and a red shirt. In "[[Breast Cancer Show Ever]]", he was seen wearing black gloves.
