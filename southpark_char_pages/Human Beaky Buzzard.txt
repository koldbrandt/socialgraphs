'''Beaky Buzzard''' is a character who appears in [[Season Fourteen]] episode, "[[Crippled Summer]]".

==Background==
He is a student at [[Lake Tardicaca|Tardicaca Camp]], which is a camp for children with disabilities. He was on the [[Blue Team]] along with [[Jimmy]], [[Timmy]], [[Lenora]], [[Elmer Fudd]], [[Droopy Dawg]], and [[Pete Puma]]. His disability makes him look and sound like the Beaky Buzzard character.

==Appearance==
He wears a blue shirt with the Camp Tardicaca logo,gray pants and black shoes. He has a long nose and a swelled head. He is also bald with two pieces of black hair coming out of the top.
