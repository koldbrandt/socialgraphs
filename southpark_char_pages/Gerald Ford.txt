'''Gerald Ford''' was the 38th President of the [[United States]] as well as the 40th Vice-president, succeeding in 1974 upon the resignation of [[Richard Nixon]]. He ran for a full term on the Republican Party ticket in 1976 but lost the election to Jimmy Carter.

An image of Ford appeared in the [[Terrance and Phillip]] documentary from the [[Season Five]] episode "[[Terrance and Phillip: Behind the Blow]]", where he is shown to be meeting with them at the [[White House]].

==Trivia==
*The meeting between Terrance and Phillip with President Gerald Ford is a reference to Tom Hanks in ''Forrest Gump'' meeting President [[John F. Kennedy]].
