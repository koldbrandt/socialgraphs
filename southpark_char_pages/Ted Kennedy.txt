'''Edward "Ted" Moore Kennedy''' was a United States Senator from Massachusetts. Ted Kennedy appeared in the episode "[[Dead Celebrities]]" on board a plane bound for [[Purgatory]], with [[Billy Mays]], [[Natasha Richardson]] and many other deceased celebrities.

==Appearance==
Ted appears as a relatively faithful portrayal of his real-life self. He has white hair and wears a black suit with a white shirt and gray tie.
