'''Kurt Fuller''' is an actor who appears in "[[Super Best Friends]]". He portrayed Karl Rove, the character he played in [[Trey Parker]] and [[Matt Stone]]'s sitcom, ''That's My Bush''.

==Appearance==
Kurt wears a blue suit, gray pants, a red tie, and oval, black-framed glasses. He has several facial wrinkles as well as a bulky, but not obese, appearance.
