The '''Female Thief''' was a member of the [[The Burger King Bandits|Burger King Bandits]]. She was shot by the police when they retook [[Pioneer Village]]. She only appeared in "[[Super Fun Time]]".

==Appearance==
She has short dirty blond hair, dirty blond eyebrows and wears pink lipstick. She also wears a black dress under a black winter jacket, gray leggings and black sandals.
