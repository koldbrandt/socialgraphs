The '''Boy with Orange Shirt''' occasionally appears in the background, primarily in large crowds, at parties, or in the [[South Park Elementary|school]] hallways. In "[[Dances with Smurfs]]", he is seen in a different classroom.

==Appearance==
Boy with Orange Shirt wears an orange shirt with brown stripes, brown pants, and blue shoes.
