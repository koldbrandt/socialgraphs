'''Fred Cartman''' is [[Liane Cartman]]'s nephew, the son of Cartman's [[Cartman's Unnamed Relatives|unnamed uncle and aunt]] and [[Eric Cartman]]'s cousin. He made his first appearance in "[[Merry Christmas Charlie Manson!]]". His only other appearance was in "[[Cartmanland]]".

==Appearance==
Fred wears a turquoise jacket, blue jeans and black shoes. He also has fairly short curly brown hair and a double chin, just like the rest of the family, he's obese. He has no other distinguishable characteristics.
