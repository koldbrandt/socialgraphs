'''Bobby''' appears in the [[Season Sixteen]] episode, "[[Faith Hilling]]".

==Background==
In "[[Faith Hilling]]", Bobby and his father walk past [[Eric Cartman]] and [[Kyle Broflovski]] "[[List of Memes|Faith Hilling]]" outside the [[Planned Parenthood Clinic]]. Bobby asks what the two were doing, and the father tells him it's before his time. Bobby comments that it is drole, and the two leave.

==Appearance==
Bobby wears black shoes, gray pants, and a white shirt with a blue tie that has red stripes. He also wears a black coat and a black cap, both of which adorn the logo of his private school, an academy. Bobby can also be seen holding a multi-colored lollipop.
