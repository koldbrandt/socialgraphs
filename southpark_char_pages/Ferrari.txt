'''Ferrari''' is an [[Raisins Girls|employee]] at [[Raisins (Location)|Raisins]]. She made her first appearance in "[[Raisins]]", in which she was the newest employee. Like all other Raisins workers, she is named after a luxury sports car manufacturer.

==Appearance==
Ferrari is seen in the standard Raisins uniform. She has long brown hair, and wears light green eyeshadow.

==Personality==
Not much is known about Ferrari's personality. Ferrari seems to listen well, as [[Mercedes]] taught her the basics.

==Prominence==
*"[[Raisins]]" - As the new girl, Mercedes teaches her the basics.
*"[[You Got F'd in the A]]" - Can be seen in the background when [[Stan Marsh|Stan]] visits Raisins.
* "[[Butterballs]]" - Seen in Stan's anti-bullying video.
