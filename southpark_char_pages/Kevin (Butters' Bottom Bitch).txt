'''Kevin''' first appears in [[Season Thirteen]]'s episode, "[[Butters' Bottom Bitch]]" as one of the Alpha Tau Omega fraternity teenagers who were arrested by [[Harrison Yates]]. He later appears in the [[Season Nineteen]] episode, "[[Stunning and Brave]]" as one of the PC Brothers.

==Appearance==
Kevin has dark brown hair and short brown eyebrows. He wears a long-sleeved blue shirt with two black stripes in the center, a black belt with gray buckle, gray pants and brown boots.
