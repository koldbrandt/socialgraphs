'''Matthew Klinner''' was the winner of the "Become a South Park Citizen" sweepstakes who appeared at the end of the [[South Park Title Sequence|title sequence]] specifically for the [[Season Fourteen]] premiere episode "[[Sexual Healing]]".

==Background==
[[File:Matthew-Klinner-Intro.png|thumb|left|140px|Matthew in intro.]]Matthew Klinner's character was animated into the opening title sequence in the ending crowd scene along with [[The Boys]], standing, waving, and smiling at the viewer next to the South Park sign. His character was created based on photographs that he sent to the South Park artists. Additionally, he was given a special poster signed by South Park creators [[Trey Parker]] and [[Matt Stone]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://southpark.cc.com/blog/2010/03/17/season-premiere-tonight|title=Season Premiere Tonight!|date=March 17, 2010}}</ref>

==Appearance==
Matthew's character has brown hair that is combed on the top and shaved short on the sides and brown eyebrows. He wears a green shirt with two white skull designs on it and blue jeans.

==Trivia==
* Matthew was the second of four ''[[South Park]]'' fans who have guest starred on the show. The other guests include:
**[[Alex Glick]], who appeared in "[[Red Man's Greed]]".
**[[Hugh]] who appeared in "[[Dead Kids]]" and "[[Nobody Got Cereal?]]".
**[[Sef Furman]], who appeared in "[[The Pandemic Special]]".
