'''Jenny''' is a kindergartner who made her first appearance in the [[Season Four]] episode, "[[Trapper Keeper]]". She made several other appearances in later episodes, usually as a background character. She speaks in "[[Trapper Keeper]]", "[[Proper Condom Use]]", and "[[Tegridy Farms]]".

==Appearance==
Jenny wears a red long-sleeved shirt with three flower designs in the middle, a blue jumpsuit and black shoes. She has shoulder-length curly black hair.
