'''John F. Kennedy Jr.''' was the son of former President of the United States, [[John F. Kennedy]]. Kennedy was seen alongside his father as a resident of [[Hell]] during the song, "[[Christmas Time in Hell]]", in "[[Mr. Hankey's Christmas Classics]]". The episode aired just six months after Kennedy's death.

==Appearance==
Kennedy appeared with a photo-realistic head, much like [[Saddam Hussein]]. He wore a purple, long-sleeve shirt with "Boston" printed slightly above its center.
