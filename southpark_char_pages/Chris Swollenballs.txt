'''Chris Swollenballs''' is a news reporter who was the lead reporter for [[Channel 4 News]] from [[Season Five]] to [[Season Nine]], working with anchor [[Tom Pusslicker]].

==Appearance==
Chris Swollenballs wears a brown suit, a white and blue shirt, and a dark red tie. He has combed blond hair and blond eyebrows.
