'''Ms. Herman''' is the primary librarian at the [[South Park Public Library]]. She was mentioned by [[Mayor McDaniels]] in "[[Conjoined Fetus Lady]]" when she was praised for having opened a new wing to her library.

==Appearance==
Ms. Herman wears black shoes, brown stockings, a maroon skirt, and an orange shirt. She also has large, teal hair and wears gray, round eyeglasses.
