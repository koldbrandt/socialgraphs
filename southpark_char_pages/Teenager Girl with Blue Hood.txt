The '''Teenager Girl with Blue Hood''' is an unnamed character who first appears in the [[Season Eight]] episode, "[[Stupid Spoiled Whore Video Playset]]", where she is seen as a whore in the [[Stupid Spoiled Whore]] store. 

==Appearance==
She has short dirty blonde hair and has thin brown eyebrows. She wears a lot of makeup consisting of a purple eyeshadow, black mascara and red lipstick, and is wearing a blue hood. On the inside of her jacket, she is wearing a yellow bra.

==Prominence==
*"[[Stupid Spoiled Whore Video Playset]]" -  She asks [[Mr. Testaburger]] to buy her a purse.
*"[[Erection Day]]" -  Seen in the background when [[Jimmy]] is about to meet Nut Gobbler.
*"[[The Ring]]" - Seen in the audience watching the [[Jonas Brothers]].
*"[[Crippled Summer]]" -  Seen walking down the street at the beginning of the episode.
*"[[REHASH|#REHASH]]" - Seen watching [[Lorde]].
*"[[Band in China]]" - Seen in the audience at the end of the episode, watching [[The Boys]]' band.
