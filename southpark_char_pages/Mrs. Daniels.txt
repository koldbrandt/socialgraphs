'''Mrs. Daniels''' is [[Nichole Daniels|Nichole]]'s mother and she first appears in the [[Season Sixteen]] episode, "[[Cartman Finds Love]]".

==Background==
In "[[Cartman Finds Love]]", Nichole and her family are eating dinner when her father brings up that Nichole has a boyfriend, [[Token Black]]. After her father teases her for dating the only other African American student, Mrs. Daniels supports Nichole and is very happy for her. 

==Appearance==
Mrs. Daniels wears black shoes, a red v-neck dress, and a light-yellow shirt. She has long, black hair and wears a gold necklace and maroon lipstick. She is slightly overweight.

Her hair has been largely trimmed since "[[The Pandemic Special]]".
