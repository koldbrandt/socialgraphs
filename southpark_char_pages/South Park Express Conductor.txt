The '''South Park Express Conductor''' is a minor character who first appears in "[[Cartman Gets an Anal Probe]]".

==Appearance==
The South Park Express conductor wears a blue trucker cap with white vertical stripes and has red hair along with a moustache. He is often seen wearing a white short sleeved shirt with a pair of overalls and has a golden chain in his right pocket.

==Prominence==
*"[[Cartman Gets an Anal Probe]]" - Informs the cows that they cannot get on his train.
*"[[Ike's Wee Wee]]" - Refuses to sell a ticket to [[Ike Broflovski]] for him to travel to Nebraska.
*"[[Prehistoric Ice Man]]" - Seen briefly when [[Stan Marsh|Stan]] and [[Kyle Broflovski|Kyle]] are fighting.
