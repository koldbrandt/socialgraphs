The '''Queen of Canada''' is a minor character who appears in [[Season Fifteen]] episode "[[Royal Pudding]]".

==Background==
The Queen appeared as one of the attendees at the royal wedding of the [[Prince and Princess of Canada]].

==Appearance==
Like most Canadians, she has beady eyes and a flapping head. She wears a gold dress and a lime green skirt, along with a gold top hat. She also visibly wears lipstick.

==Trivia==
*The Queen's appearance matches those of [[Queen Elizabeth II]] at her attendance in the Wedding of Prince William and Catherine Middleton on April 29, 2011.
