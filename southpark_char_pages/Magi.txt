The '''Magi''', also known as the '''Three Wise Men''', are a group of religious figures known for visiting [[Jesus Christ]] after his birth, bringing gifts of gold, frankincense, and myrrh. They appears in "[[The Spirit of Christmas#Jesus vs. Frosty|Jesus vs. Frosty]]", as well as [[Season Three]]'s [[Mr. Hankey's Christmas Classics]]".

==Background==
During the Nativity scene in "Jesus vs. Frosty", [[Stan Marsh|Stan]] and [[Kyle Broflovski|Kyle]] approaches Jesus who is surrounded by the Magi, seeking his help to destroy [[Frosty the Snowman]].

In "[[Mr. Hankey's Christmas Classics]]", as [[Eric Cartman]] is singing "[[O Holy Night]]", he is seen taking the gifts from the Magi and loading them up a camel.

==Appearance==
The Magi wears headdresses and robes of varying colors, each carrying one of the three gifts.
