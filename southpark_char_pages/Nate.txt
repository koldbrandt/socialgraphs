'''Nate''' is a minor character and a student at [[South Park Elementary]]. He has appeared several times as a background, or filler, character.

==Appearance==
He wears black shoes, gray pants, and a dark green coat with a brown collar. He has orange curly hair.

==Trivia==
*He was named "Tommy" in "[[Are You There God? It's Me, Jesus]]". He is also often referred to as "Carrot" by fans of the show due to his orange hair.
*Despite his orange curly hair, he is not considered a "[[Ginger Kids|Ginger Kid]]". The same can be said about [[Red McArthur|Red]] and [[Dougie O'Connell|Dougie]].
