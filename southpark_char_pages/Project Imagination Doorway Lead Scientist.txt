The '''[[Project Imagination Doorway]] Lead Scientist''' is an American scientist working for the [[Pentagon]] for 40 years, under [[General Deckter]].

==Appearance==
This character is based upon the model of [[Mitch]], which was redesigned to have a balding brown hair.

==Prominence==
*"[[Two Days Before the Day After Tomorrow]]" - Works as a senior geologist in the town of [[South Park (Location)|South Park]].
*"[[Imaginationland]]" - Takes the role of chief Scientist tasked with finding [[Imaginationland (Location)|Imaginationland]].
*"[[Imaginationland, Episode II]]" - Killed by [[ManBearPig]], who invaded reality from Imaginationland.
*"[[Imaginationland, Episode III]]" - His death is seen in footage multiple times.
