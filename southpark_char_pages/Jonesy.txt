'''Jonesy''' is a musician and a member of [[Skyler]]'s band in "[[Cat Orgy]]" and "[[Timmy 2000]]". He first appears in the [[Season Two]] episodes "[[Clubhouses]]" and "[[Chef Aid]]".

==Appearance==
Jonesy has long black hair. He wears black pants and a green shirt. In later episodes his shirt has a Zildjian logo on it.

<gallery widths="160" captionalign="left" caption="Images of Jonesy">
Jonesy.jpg|Jonesy at the drums.
</gallery>

==Personality==
Jonesy seems to have a negative attitude, even insulting himself and the other band members in "[[Timmy 2000]]".

==Prominence==
*"[[Clubhouses]]" -  He and his friend [[Scott ("Clubhouses")|Scott]] are invited by the [[Runaway Girls|runaway girls]] to the Cartman club house.
*"[[Chef Aid]]" - Attends Chef Aid.
*"[[Cat Orgy]]" -  Plays with the rest of Skyler's band at Cartman's house.
*"[[Timmy 2000]]" - Allows Timmy to join the band and competes in the Battle of the Bands.
*"[[How to Eat with Your Butt]]" - Asks Cartman why he isn't laughing at the movies.
*"[[Christian Rock Hard]]" - Jonesy and the band follow suit after [[Moop]] strikes.

==Trivia==
*Jonesy wears a T-shirt which reads, "[[Wikipedia:Avedis Zildjian Company|Zildjian]]" which is a reference to a company of the same name which supplies music to the show.
