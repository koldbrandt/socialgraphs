'''Popeye''' appeared as a member of the [[Council of Nine]] in the Imaginationland trilogy. He was seen fighting against some of the evil characters, including the [[Woodland Critters]], where he was getting raped by them until Butters imagined some spinach for him. Popeye's theme song also plays whenever [[Towelie (Character)|Towelie]] gets high. He has not appeared since the trilogy.

==Appearance==
Popeye appears very similar to his cartoon likeness. He is bald, he squints and has a large chin with a large overbite. He has large muscles with anchor tattoos on his forceps. He can be seen wearing a brown hat, a black sailor outfit with white trimming and gold buttons, and sky blue pants. He also smokes a pipe.
