'''Alfredo James Pacino''', better known professionally as '''Al Pacino''', is an American actor most famous for his roles in ''The Godfather'' and ''Scarface''. He appears in the [[Season Eighteen]] episode, "[[HappyHolograms|#HappyHolograms]]".

==Background==
He was mentioned in "[[Ass Burgers]]" as a character in the [[Adam Sandler]] film ''Jack and Jill', with [[Stan Marsh]] laughing in the theater when Sandler's character proclaims that Al Pacino "wants to play twista with your sista'.

He appeared as one of the celebrity guests on the [[Washington Redskins: Go Fuck Yourself Holiday Special]].

==Appearance==
Al Pacino wears a plain black suit with a red scarf around his around. He has messy black hair and a goatee. He is also visibly aged.
