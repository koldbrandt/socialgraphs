'''Hannah Williams''' is a first grader at [[South Park Elementary]] who first appears in the [[Season Nine]] episode, "[[Erection Day]]". Hannah's largest role was in "[[Put It Down]]", when she performed at the school talent show after [[Tweek Tweak|Tweek]]. She sang "[[The Wheels on the Bus]]" while crying because Tweek's performance before her had scared her.

==Appearance==
Hannah is an adorable first grader with wavy black hair. She wears a yellow shirt with green sleeves, brown pants, and has a flower in her hair.

==Personality==
Hannah is sensitive to heavy news. When [[Tweek Tweak|Tweek]] announced to the audience at the school talent show that [[North Korea]] was threatening the U.S., she cried, terrified by his warnings.
