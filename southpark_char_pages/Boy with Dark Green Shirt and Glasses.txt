The '''Boy With Dark Green Shirt and Glasses''' is a background character who appears in [[Season Three]] and [[Season Four]].

==Appearance==
He wears big black glasses, a dark green shirt, brown pants, matching gloves, and black shoes. It is unknown if he has black hair or if he is wearing a black hat of some sort.

==Prominence==
*"[[Tweek vs. Craig]]" - Watches the fight between [[Tweek Tweak|Tweek]] and [[Craig Tucker|Craig]].
*''[[South Park: Bigger, Longer & Uncut]]'' - Watches "[[Asses of Fire]]" in [[Bijou Cinema]].
*"[[Chinpokomon]]" - Seen buying [[Chinpokomon (Brand)|Chinpokomon dolls]] at [[Luau's Toys]].
*"[[Hooked on Monkey Fonics]]" - Seen at the dance.
*"[[Cartman's Silly Hate Crime 2000]]" - Seen watching the sled race.
*"[[Probably]]" - Seen at [[Eric Cartman|Cartman]]'s meeting.
*"[[Trapper Keeper]]" - Seen at the [[South Park Elementary Playground|playground]].

==Trivia==
*He is known by fans as "Glassy".
*In "[[Jewbilee]]", one of the [[Jew Scouts|Jew Scout]] inductees looks identical to him, minus the glasses.
