'''Daniel Tanner''' occasionally appears in the background, primarily in large crowds, at parties, or in the [[South Park Elementary|school]] hallways. In "[[Skank Hunt]]", his former girlfriend, [[Annie Knitts]], breaks up with him at the end of the episode.

==Appearance==
Daniel wears a gray shirt, blue pants, and black shoes. He has brown permed hair and most notably wears a blue hoodie jacket.

==Relationships==
===[[Annie Knitts]]===
Some time prior to the events of "[[Skank Hunt]]", he and Annie form a relationship.
