The '''New York Boy with Blue Cap''' is a character who appears in the [[Season Three]] episode, "[[World Wide Recorder Concert]]", where he is seen as one of the friends of the [[New York Kids]] [[Smitty|leader]]. He is also one of several cut characters from ''[[South Park: The Stick of Truth]]'' and he is seen on a poster at [[Raisins (Location)|Raisins]] in ''[[South Park: The Fractured But Whole]]''.

==Appearance==
He has dark Brown hair and wears a light Blue cap with beige edges, a White shirt under a dark Blue Jacket with Red stripes on the sides and Blue pants. In his model found in the game files, he showed a slightly different appearance.

In later episodes, he wears a White jersey with the number 34 on it.

==Prominence==
*"[[World Wide Recorder Concert]]" - He and the other [[New York Kids]] antagonize the boys during the episode.
*"[[The Entity]]" - He moves to South Park and is briefly mistaken for [[Kyle Broflovski|Kyle]]'s cousin.
*"[[Raisins]]" - Seen on a poster at Raisins and also physically appears at one of the tables.
*"[[You Got F'd in the A]]" - Seen on a poster at [[Raisins (Location)|Raisins]].
*"[[AWESOM-O]]" - Seen with his parents.

==Trivia==
*Like Smitty, he also appears in the [[Raisins (Location)|Raisins restaurant]] on a poster.
*In the ''[[South Park: The Stick of Truth]]'' game files, his character model is described as "newyork_1".
