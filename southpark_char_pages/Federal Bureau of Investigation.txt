The '''Federal Bureau of Investigation''', also known as the '''F.B.I.''', is a [[United States]] law enforcement agency. The F.B.I. handles incidents on a national level, as opposed to local law enforcement agencies, who only handle incidents on a city and county level. The F.B.I. has been featured in many episodes, first appearing in the [[Season One]] episode, "[[Starvin' Marvin]]".

==Appearance==
The F.B.I. are depicted as mysterious characters, closely resembling their real counterparts. The are seen wearing black suits, black ties, black sunglasses, and black hats. They are also shown to have little personality.
