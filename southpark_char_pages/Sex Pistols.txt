An image of the '''Sex Pistols''' with [[Jerome "Chef" McElroy|Chef]] appears in "[[Chef Aid]]", in Chef's photo album. John Lydon (also known as Johnny Rotten), the lead singer, also narrates the accompanying mockumentary ''[[Chef Aid: Behind the Menu]]''.

One of their songs, "[[Pretty Vacant]]", is played by [[Stan Marsh|Stan]] and [[Kyle Broflovski|Kyle]] on ''[[Guitar Hero]]'' in "[[Guitar Queer-O]]".
