'''Jada Pinkett Smith''' appears with her husband, [[Will Smith]], in the [[Season Five]] episode, "[[Here Comes the Neighborhood]]". They decide to build a house in [[South Park (Location)|South Park]] and move there citing South Park as a "beautiful unspoiled town in the Rockies". Jada compares South Park to what [[Aspen]] was like 30 years prior.

==Appearance==
Jada Pinkett Smith wears a blue business suit, a white formal shirt, and large gold hoop earrings. She has short black hair and wears a conservative amount of makeup, which consists of red lipstick and black eyelashes.
