'''Michael Buffer''', voiced by himself, appears as the ringside announcer in the [[Season One]] episode, "[[Damien]]".

==Background==
In "[[Damien]]", he delivers his trademark "Let's Get Ready to Rumble!" line upon announcing the fight between [[Satan]] and [[Jesus Christ]].

==Appearance==
As he usually appears when he announces boxing matches, Michael Buffer wears a black tuxedo consisting of a black coat, white formal shirt, black pants, and a black bow tie. Although in ''South Park'' his character appears with short brown hair, in reality, he has gray hair.
