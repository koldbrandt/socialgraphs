'''Don Heisman''' is a talent agent from the ''Creative Arts Agency'' (CAA) based in [[Los Angeles]]. He first appears in [[Season Nine]] episode "[[Wing]]".

==Background==
Following [[Token Black|Token's]] music performance at the ''Miss Colorado Pageant'' in [[Denver Convention Center]], Don Heisman approached Token and the boys (who were heading the [[Super Awesome Talent Agency]]) and offered Token to sign up with the CAA with shows in Los Angeles already lined up, to which Token agreed. Both Heisman and Token departed in a limousine, leaving the boys without talents for their own.

He was seen in "[[Butters' Bottom Bitch]]" as the manager of [[Association of Community Organizations for Reform Now|ACORN]], being addressed by [[Butters Stotch|Butters]] as "Mr. Daniels".

==Appearance==
Don Heisman appears with a neat, combed black hair with a mullet. He wears a navy blue business suit with a blue spotted tie.
