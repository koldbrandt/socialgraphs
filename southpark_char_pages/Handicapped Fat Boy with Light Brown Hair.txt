The '''Handicapped Fat Boy with Light Brown Hair''' is a student at [[South Park Elementary]] who appears in several episodes with other disabled children. He made his first appearance on the episode "[[Up the Down Steroid]]", where he is seen training for the special Olympics. He had a most notable appearance on "[[Crippled Summer]]", where he is seen as a member of the [[Red Team]]. For now his only important role was to have participated in the canoe race, along with [[Nathan]], [[Chad Handler]], [[Francis (Special Ed)|Francis]], [[Mimsy]] and another unnamed boy.

==Appearance==
He has short, spiky light brown hair, big ears and is usually seen with his mouth open. His most notable feature is that his eyes are rotated downward, making him look like an alien. He is also a little overweight.
