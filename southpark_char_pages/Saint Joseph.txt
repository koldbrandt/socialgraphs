'''Saint Joseph''' appeared briefly in the background, alongside [[Mary, Mother of Jesus]], during the song, "[[O Holy Night]]", in the [[Season Three]] episode, "[[Mr. Hankey's Christmas Classics]]".

==Appearance==
Saint Joseph wears a long white robe with a red scarf. He has long brown hair and has noticeable signs of aging.
