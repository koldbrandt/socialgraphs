'''Brian Boitano''' is an Olympic gold-medalist figure skater, whose professional career has spanned two decades. He has been repeatedly referenced in ''[[South Park]]'', usually preceded by the catchphrase "What would Brian Boitano do?" He is a fictionalized version of the celebrity, one of few to be portrayed in a positive light.

==Appearance==
Brian has small eyes, as well as a generally small head. He wears a red, v-neck shirt, white blouse, black pants, and dark gray ice skates.

==Personality==
He is portrayed as kind, caring, and knowledgeable. He is also heroic, saving the world from various dangers during the song "[[What Would Brian Boitano Do?]]" in ''[[South Park: Bigger, Longer & Uncut]]''.

==Trivia==
[[File:BrainTwitter.jpg|thumb|The icon for Brian's Twitter]]
*Brian Boitano was the first celebrity to appear on South Park.
*Boitano provided the foreword to the book ''[[South Park Guide to Life]]''.
*Brian Boitano now has a cooking show on the Food Network entitled ''What Would Brian Boitano Make?'', a clear reference to the song "[[What Would Brian Boitano Do?]]".
*In ''South Park'', it is believed he is capable of breathing fire and time travel, among other things.
*Despite being portrayed in a positive light, he still appeared alongside the many other celebrities who got ridiculed in "[[200]]" and "[[201]]".
*An image of the South Park version of Brian is used as his Twitter account's profile picture.

==Prominence==
*''[[The Spirit of Christmas#Jesus vs. Santa|The Spirit of Christmas: Jesus vs. Santa]]'' - At one point, [[Stan Marsh|Stan]] wonders: "[[What Would Brian Boitano Do?]]" Brian Boitano miraculously appears and gives a speech about how Christmas should be about being good to each other.
*"[[Big Gay Al's Big Gay Boat Ride]]:" - Brian Boitano is seen skating among the dancing figures during the boat ride.
*''[[South Park: Bigger, Longer & Uncut]]'' - An entire song is built around the catchphrase "[[What Would Brian Boitano Do?]]". As a result, they decide to form [[La Resistance (Organization)|La Resistance]] and go rescue [[Terrance and Phillip]].
*"[[A Very Crappy Christmas]]" (cameo) - A Brian Boitano cut-out can be seen when the boys visit [[Butters Stotch|Butters]].
*"[[200]]" (cameo) - Seen with the other celebrities.
*"[[201]]" (cameo) - Seen with the other celebrities.
