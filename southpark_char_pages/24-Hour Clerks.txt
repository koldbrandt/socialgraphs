The '''24-Hour Clerks''' are convenience store workers who first appeared in [[Season Eleven]] episode, "[[With Apologies to Jesse Jackson]]".

They are first employed in [[Day Time 24 Night Time]], and later in [[Kum & Go]] since "[[A Nightmare on Face Time]]".

==Appearance==
The clerks wear red collared shirts as uniforms. It bears the Day Time 24 Night Time logo on the right chest, even after moving to Kum & Go.

==Prominence==
*"[[With Apologies to Jesse Jackson]]" - The store clerks turn [[Randy Marsh]] away from the store, saying that he is not welcomed.
*"[[Faith Hilling]]" - The Asian clerk sells a copy of the Denver Post to [[the boys]].
*"[[A Nightmare on Face Time]]" - The Asian clerk was shot dead by [[The Redbox Killers]] following a robbery attempt.
*"[[Mexican Joker]]" - [[Eric Cartman]] speaks to the white clerk about the problems his generation has inherited.

==Trivia==
*A man who looks similar to the Asian clerk appears in "[[Le Petit Tourette]]", where he shoots himself upon seeing [[Chris Hansen]] on the latter's talk show set.
