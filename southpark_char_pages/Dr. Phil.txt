'''Phillip Calvin McGraw''', better known as '''Dr. Phil''', is an American television personality who hosted his eponymous talk show in the [[Season Fifteen]] episode, "[[HUMANCENTiPAD]]". He helped [[Eric Cartman]], who thought his mother had "fucked" him by talking to Eric about his problem and rewarding him. He was able to give Eric the first [[HUMANCENTiPAD (Product)|HUMANCENTiPAD]].

==Appearance==
Dr. Phil is mostly bald with gray hair on the sides and back of his head with gray eyebrows and a gray mustache. He wears a black suit with a light blue button-up shirt and a blue tie. He also wears black shoes.
