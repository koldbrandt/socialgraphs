'''Patty''' is a citizen of [[South Park (Location)|South Park]] who appears in several episodes from previous seasons. In "[[The Wacky Molestation Adventure]]" it is shown that she has two children and a husband.

==Appearance==
Patty has short brown hair, red lipstick and wears a green jacket and a red skirt.
