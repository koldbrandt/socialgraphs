'''Sia''' is a student at [[South Park Elementary]] who first appears in the [[Season Eighteen]] episode, "[[The Cissy]]". Although no other character refers to her by name in the episode, her voice credit is listed as "Sia".

==Background==
She appears in "[[The Cissy]]", holding her nose and begging [[Eric Cartman]] to leave the [[South Park Elementary Bathrooms|girls' bathroom]].

==Appearance==
She has very pale short blonde hair with bangs, and wears a short black jacket over a black-and-white striped shirt, with a red skirt. Her hairstyle and name may be a homage to singer Sia, who provides [[Lorde]]'s singing voice in the same episode.

==Personality==
Sia exhibits frustration and anger at Cartman's refusal to leave the girls' bathroom.
