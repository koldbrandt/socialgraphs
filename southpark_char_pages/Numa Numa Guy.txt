'''Gary Brolsma,''' better known as''' Numa Numa Guy''', appeared in the [[Season Twelve]] episode, "[[Canada on Strike]]", as one of the internet celebrities waiting for their theoretical payments. He is famous for lip-syncing to the song "[[Dragostea Din Tei]]" while performing a distinctive dance.

==Background==
During "[[Canada on Strike]]", he fought [[Tron Guy]] while singing, but was eventually shredded up by [[Sneezing Panda]]'s mother. He is also seen on the internet in "[[Crack Baby Athletic Association]]."

==Appearance==
He appeared visibly overweight, wore a white shirt, headphones, glasses, and has brown hair.

==Trivia==
*He and [[Dramatic Look Gopher]] are the only Internet characters that are accompanied by their original music.
*The image of him fighting against the [[Sneezing Panda]]'s mother in the episode was used as a header image of his official Twitter account.
