'''Blaze''' is a stripper who works at [[The Peppermint Hippo]]. She is well known for her catchphrases "Would you like a dance?", and "Dance?! Anybody like a dance?". She first appears in the [[Season Seven]] episode, "[[Lil' Crime Stoppers]]". She is later seen working in [[South Park Elementary]]'s Administration Office in "[[Tegridy Farms]]".

==Appearance==
Blaze has brown hair and makeup that consists of purple and blue eyeshadow, mascara, and red lipstick. She is wearing purple lingerie that has fur on it and white heels. 

==Prominence==
*"[[Lil' Crime Stoppers]]" – Debut.
*"[[Erection Day]]" – Crying and clapping near the end.
*"[[Bloody Mary]]" – Sitting with the kids at the police station.
*"[[The Return of Chef]]" – Seen performing at [[The Peppermint Hippo]].
*"[[Tegridy Farms]]" – Working as [[PC Principal]]'s secretary.
*"[[Buddha Box]]" – Voice only appearance, tells PC Princial that Vice Principal [[Strong Woman]] needs him.
*"[[South ParQ Vaccination Special]]" - Seen attending [[Margaret Nelson]]'s funeral, wearing a green dress.

==''[[South Park: The Fractured But Whole]]''==
In [[The Bowels of the Beast]] mission, she appears briefly during [[The New Kid]] and [[Captain Diabetes]]' fight against the [[VIP Johns]], peeking into the room and asking for a dance. She is later involved in a fight against the two superheroes, keeping them away from [[Classi]].

===Abilities===
*'''Stripper Rush''' - Charges past, then slams on the opponent for reverse knockback.
*'''Thunder Down Under''' - A ranged attack.

==Trivia==
*Her name was revealed in ''[[South Park: The Fractured But Whole]]''.
