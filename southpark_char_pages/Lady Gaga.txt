'''Stefani Joanne Angelina Germanotta''', better known as '''Lady Gaga''', is an American singer known for her songs such as "Poker Face" and "Bad Romance". She appears in the [[Season Eighteen]] episode, "[[HappyHolograms|#HappyHolograms]]".

==Background==
Lady Gaga was seen singing Christmas songs on stage with [[Tony Bennett]] in [[Washington_Redskins:_Go_Fuck_Yourself_Holiday_Special|Washington Redskins: Go Fuck Yourself Holiday Special]].

==Appearance==
Lady Gaga wears a white gown with a veil and also a puffy white hat on her head.

==Trivia==
*Her 2008 single, "Poker Face" was parodied by [[Eric Cartman]] in "[[Whale Whores]]" as "[[Poker Face ft. Cartman]]".
