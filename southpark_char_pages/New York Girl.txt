The '''New York Girl''' is a member of the [[New York Kids]] who had two physical appearances on the show, the first in the episode "[[Roger Ebert Should Lay off the Fatty Foods]]" and the other in "[[World Wide Recorder Concert]]", where she is seen with several other kids from New York.

She and three other New York kids can be seen on some posters at [[Raisins (Location)|Raisins]]. She is also one of several cut characters from ''[[South Park: The Stick of Truth]]''.

==Appearance==
She has short curly Orange hair with a Pink headband, gold earrings and wears a Black shirt and light Blue pants.
