The '''Crippled Girl with Brown Hair''' is a handicapped girl who studies at [[South Park Elementary]]. She made her first appearance on "[[Up the Down Steroid]]" and later appeared on the episodes "[[Crippled Summer]]" and "[[Moss Piglets]]". For the time being she has played no notable role.

==Appearance==
She has long brown hair, brown eyebrows and is usually seen looking up. She also uses a gray wheelchair. In "[[Crippled Summer]]" she wore a standard red Tardicaca Camp shirt, a purple skirt and pink sandals, outside the camp she appears to be wearing a light pink dress. She seems to have exotropy.

==Prominence==
*"[[Up the Down Steroid]]" - Seen training for the special Olympics.
*"[[Crippled Summer]]" - Seen as a member of the [[Red Team]].
*"[[Moss Piglets]]" - Seen at the science fair.
