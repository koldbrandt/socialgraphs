'''Grandpa McCormick''' appears in the [[Season Four]] episode "[[Fat Camp]]". He did not have any speaking lines but he received a full body sensual massage from his grandson Kenny. He is laying on a heart-shaped bed in front of a studio audience when Kenny films this stunt.

==Appearance==
Grandpa McCormick has gray hair and is visibly aged. He is seen only in his light blue underpants and his gray under shirt.
