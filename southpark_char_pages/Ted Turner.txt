'''Ted Turner''' is the media mogul behind the Turner empire. He is one of the select rich and famous people [[Herbert Garrison|Mr. Garrison]] invites to view [[IT]] in the [[Season Five]] episode "[[The Entity]]". This was his only appearance to date.

==Appearance==
Turner has white hair and wears a black suit with a white shirt and grey tie. He appears on the scene in a private helicopter.
