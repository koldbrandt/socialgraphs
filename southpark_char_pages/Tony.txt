'''Tony''' is a [[Hopeful Hills]] camper who appears in the [[Season Four]] episode, "[[Fat Camp]]".

==Background==
[[File:Tony.png|thumb|left|Tony about to take his order.]]
Tony is seen when [[Eric Cartman|Cartman]] is selling candy to campers while the [[Rick and Susan Tyler|counselors]] are not watching them. After Cartman hands two donuts to a camper, Tony appears and Cartman asks him if he wanted the usual. Tony doesn't answer but nods at Cartman, so Cartman gives Tony a packet of cookies. Tony is later seen at the end of the episode when campers reveal that Cartman was selling candy to them.

==Appearance==
Tony is much overweight and has combed brown hair and a double chin, his eyes are tightly narrowed and he also has some wrinkles under his eyes. He wears the standard Fat Camp uniform consisting of a white short-sleeved shirt with the Fat Camp logo and blue shorts. Outside the camp he wears a light green short-sleeved shirt and light brown pants.
