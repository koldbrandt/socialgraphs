The '''Man with Blond Hair and Gray Shirt''' is an unnamed resident of [[South Park (Location)|South Park]] who first appears in the [[Season Two]] episode, "[[Chef Aid]]".

==Appearance==
The man has short Blond hair and wears a Gray shirt and dark Green pants.
