'''Kelly Turner''' is [[Sally Turner]]'s mother. She was first seen in the [[Season One]] episode, "[[Tom's Rhinoplasty]]" and typically appears in the background.

==Appearance==
Kelly has long blonde hair. She wears yellow/orange sweater and green trousers.
