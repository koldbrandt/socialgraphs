'''Emily Marx''' is a student at [[South Park Elementary]]. She first appear at [[Heidi Turner]]'s slumber party in "[[Marjorine]]", and later at the [[Jonas Brothers]] concert in "[[The Ring]]". Her name was confirmed in "[[Bass to Mouth]]".

==Appearance==
Emily has light blonde hair and wears a dark blue jacket and light green pants. In earlier appearances, she wore a navy blue jacket with blue-grey trousers. In "[[Marjorine]]", she wears a turquoise pajama top with green and orange hearts, and green pants.

==Personality==
She seems to be relatively sensitive to social criticism, considering in "[[Bass to Mouth]]" she was embarrassed, not because her mother was in rehab, but because the other girls might find out.

==Relationships==
===[[Tammy Nelson]]===
It was shown in "[[Bass to Mouth]]" that her and Tammy are very close friends, probably best friends, considering they both admitted and comforted each other over their mothers being addicts/alcoholics, a very personal matter. They are also seen sitting together in the school gymnasium in "[[Cock Magic]]". At the Board Girls club, they are playing a marvel game together.

===[[Jason White]]===
In "[[Skank Hunt]]", Emily is seen breaking up with Jason toward the end of the episode. Their relationship is over regardless, as Jason died in "[[Season Finale]]".

*In the [[Eavesdropper]] story about her and [[Tammy Nelson]]'s mothers, she was referred to as "Jen Marx".
