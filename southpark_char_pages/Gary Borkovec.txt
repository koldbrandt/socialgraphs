'''Gary Borkovec''' was a student at [[South Park Elementary]] who appeared in the [[Season Twenty-One]] episode, "[[Put It Down]]" where he was shown trying to raise awareness of distracted driving. Ironically, he was later hit and killed by a car being operated by a distracted driver on her phone.

==Appearance==
Gary was a fourth grader with light brown hair and buck teeth. He wore a striped orange shirt, brown pants, and a blue vest. He also had unusually longer arms than most other fourth graders in [[South Park (Location)|South Park]].
