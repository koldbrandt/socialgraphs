'''Peter''' is a minor character that appears in the [[Season Two]] episode, "[[Roger Ebert Should Lay off the Fatty Foods]]", singing "[[Bingo]]" as a part of an audition to make an appearance in a [[Cheesy Poofs]] commercial.

==Appearance==
Peter has messy red hair and freckles on his face. He wears a light green shirt with a red collar, blue pants, and black shoes.
