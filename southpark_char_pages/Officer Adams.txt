'''Mitchell Adams''', or '''Officer Adams''', is a police officer in [[Park County Police Station]]. He first appears in [[Season Twelve]] episode "[[The China Probrem]]".

==Appearance==
Officer Adams wears the standard police uniform. He has light brown hair and features a protruding chin.

==Prominence==
*"[[The China Probrem]]" - Involved in a standoff at [[P.F. Chang's]].
*"[[A Nightmare on Face Time]]" - Seen arresting [[The Redbox Killers]].
*"[[HappyHolograms|#HappyHolograms]]" - Reported to [[Harrison Yates]] that he had arrested and attempted to choke [[Michael Jackson Hologram]]. His named was revealed to be "Mitchell Adams".

==''[[South Park: The Fractured But Whole]]''==
Officer Adams is one of several police officers that are fought during [[The Thin White Line]] mission.

===Abilities===
*'''Taser''' - Fires a taser gun. Inflicts Shocked status.
*'''Pepper Spray''' - Delivers a dose of pepper spray. Inflicts Grossed Out status.
