'''John D'Aquino '''is an American actor, who appeared in the [[Season Five]] episode, "[[Super Best Friends]]", as [[George Bush|George Bush's]] neighbor, '''Larry'''. 

As Bush decides whether to give the [[David Blaine|Blaintologists]] tax-exempt status or let them commit ritual suicide, Larry randomly walks in and says "Hey George!".

==Appearance==
He appeared in a light blue shirt and brown pants.

==Trivia==
*The character Larry is from [[Trey Parker]] and [[Matt Stone]]'s sitcom about the White House, ''That's My Bush''. The sitcom was also heavily referenced in "[[Mystery of the Urinal Deuce]]".
