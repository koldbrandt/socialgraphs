'''Christopher Darren Cunningham''', better known by the stage name as '''Chris Crocker''', is an American internet celebrity notable for his "Leave [[Britney Spears|Britney]] Alone" video who appeared in the [[Season Twelve]] episode, "[[Canada on Strike]]". 

==Background==
He appeared in the episode "[[Canada on Strike]]" as one of the internet celebrities waiting for their theoretical payments. During the bloody brawl between the internet celebrities, he was mauled to death by [[Sneezing Panda]]'s mother for constantly screaming "Leave (someone) alone!", in reference to his YouTube video.

==Appearance==
Chris Crocker has black hair and trimmed, black eyebrows. He has blond hair highlights and wears a white T-shirt with black trimming. He also has on makeup which is runny because he is crying, as seen in the video.

==Personality==
Crocker is highly melodramatic and over-emotional, reacting hysterically to even the slightest criticism of anyone, demanding people "Leave (someone) alone!" constantly. As such, he appears to enjoy receiving attention for his dramatic antics. Crocker is openly gay in real life and is very effeminate in his mannerisms and behavior.
