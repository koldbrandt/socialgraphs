'''William Daniels''' is [[Nichole Daniels|Nichole]]'s father and he first appears in the [[Season Sixteen]] episode, "[[Cartman Finds Love]]".

==Background==
In "[[Cartman Finds Love]]", Nichole and her family are eating dinner when her father brings up that Nichole has a boyfriend, [[Token Black]]. Although he is happy for her, he subtly teases her for gravitating to the only other African American student at [[South Park Elementary]]. Nichole's mother interjects by finding nothing wrong with that and encouraging Nichole.

==Appearance==
William wears black shoes, khaki pants with a black belt, and a teal polo shirt. He has black hair, and a full beard and mustache.
