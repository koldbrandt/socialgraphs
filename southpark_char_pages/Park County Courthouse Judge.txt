The '''[[Park County Courthouse]] Judge''' is an unnamed woman who, as her title suggests, is the judge at the [[Park County Courthouse]]. She appears in the [[Season Twenty-One]] episode, "[[White People Renovating Houses]]", judging the case between [[Randy Marsh]] and [[Darryl Weathers]].

==Appearance==
The judge is an elderly, heavy-set woman with wavy gray hair. She wears a black judicial robe with a white undershirt. She also wears glasses, pink lipstick, and gold earrings.
