The '''Cameraman''' is a character that was quite prominent in the early seasons and first appear in the [[Season One]] episode, "[[Weight Gain 4000]]".

==Appearance==
The Cameraman has some long messy blond hair and wears a dark green shirt under a brown vest, dark brown pants, and black shoes. He was usually seen with a slight smile on his face. The Cameraman at his first appearance was seen wearing a dark red shirt and pale brown pants.
