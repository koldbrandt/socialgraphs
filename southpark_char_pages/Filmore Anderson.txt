'''Filmore Anderson''' is a boy in [[Kindergartners|kindergarten]]. He has often been a rival to [[Ike Broflovski]], but they appear sometimes as friends. He is often accompanied by his best friend [[Quaid]].

==Appearance==
Filmore has short, spiky black hair. He wears a red shirt with a dump truck on it, and jeans. His name invokes that of [[Al Gore]], whom he was a stand-in for in his first appearance, "[[Trapper Keeper]]". According to the same episode, Filmore's aunt is [[Rosie O'Donnell]], whom he slightly resembles.

===''[[South Park: The Stick of Truth]]''===
Filmore appears as a quest giver for [[Hide 'n' Seek]]. He gives the player a quest to play Hide and Seek with the Kindergarteners. He can be found hiding behind the desk in the bank.

===''[[South Park: The Fractured But Whole]]''===
Filmore can be found playing at the [[Community Park]] playground. If the New Kid has [[Ike Broflovski]] as a Coonstagram follower, they will be able to take a selfie with Filmore as well.
