'''Robert William Flay''' is an American celebrity chef who appears in [[Season Fourteen]] episode "[[Crème Fraiche]]".

==Background==
Upon hearing that [[Randy Marsh]] had taken over the role as [[South Park Elementary]]'s school chef which caught the attention of "Gordon Ramsey" (who was actually an alter-ego of [[Eric Cartman]]), Bobby Flay heads to the school cafeteria, much to the excitement of Randy, and initiated a cooking challenge along with several other celebrity chefs against Randy.

==Appearance==
Bobby Flay has red hair and several freckles on his cheek. He wears the standard chef's uniform.
