'''Leslie''' is one of the geniuses who worked for the [[Apple Inc.|Apple]] Store in the episode "[[HUMANCENTiPAD]]" and helped get [[Kyle Broflovski|Kyle]] back from Apple's headquarters. Having not read the [[Apple Inc.#Terms & Conditions|Terms &amp; Conditions]], Kyle was taken to the [[Apple HQ]] to become a part of the [[HUMANCENTiPAD (Product)|HUMANCENTiPAD]]. [[Stan Marsh]] and [[Gerald Broflovski]] went to the Apple Store to speak with a genius to undo the contract. Leslie had a "quickening" with the other geniuses as was able to undo the binding of the contract.

==Appearance==
Leslie had brown hair with subtle gray highlights. She wears traditional Apple Store employee attire; black pants, a blue shirt with a white atom logo, and a white Apple Store employee badge hanging from her neck. Leslie is slightly overweight.
