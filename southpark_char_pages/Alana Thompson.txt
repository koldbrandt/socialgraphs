'''Alana "Honey Boo Boo" Thompson''' is a reality television personality and frequent beauty pageant contestant who appears in the [[Season Sixteen]] episode, "[[Raising the Bar]]". She is the star of the reality television show, ''[[Here Comes Honey Boo Boo]]''. She is mentioned in "[[A Nightmare on Face Time]]" where a man mistakes [[Eric Cartman|Cartman]]'s Hulk costume for a green Honey Boo Boo.

==Appearance==
Alana is overweight, has blonde hair, and wears a pink tank top and blue pants. At beauty pageants, she wears a dress of various shades of pink, purple, and white and wears excessive amounts of makeup.
