The '''[[Michael Jackson]] Fan''' is an African-American audience member of "[[Jesus and Pals]]" who appeared in "[[The Mexican Staring Frog of Southern Sri Lanka]]". He repeatedly defends [[Michael Jackson]] for the allegations of child abuse.

==Appearance==
The Michael Jackson fan is a middle-aged African-American who dresses and sports the same hairdo as Michael Jackson in "Thriller".
