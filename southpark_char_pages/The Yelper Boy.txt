The '''Yelper Boy''' is the son of [[Enchorito Mark]] who first appears in "[[Erection Day]]".

==Background==
In his first appearance, he and his parents have lunch at [[Whistlin' Willy's]]. He threatens to give the restaurant just one star because [[Whistlin' Willy (Mascot)|Willy]] the mascot has not sung "Happy Birthday" to him yet.

==Appearance==
The boy has curly brown hair and wears a white shirt with a red truck under a green jacket and light brown pants. In "[[Insecurity]]", he wears a light blue jacket.

==Prominence==
*"[[Erection Day]]" - Seen in the school hallways.
*"[[Insecurity]]" - Seen with his mother in a shoe store, where the [[Shoe Saver Salesman|salesman]] threatens to kill him.
*"[[Black Friday]]" - Seen in [[Stan Marsh|Stan]]'s army.
*"[[You're Not Yelping]]"
*"[[Bike Parade]]" - Seen watching the Bike Parade.
