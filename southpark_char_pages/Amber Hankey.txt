'''Amber Hankey''' is one of [[Mr. Hankey]]'s children. She appears in the [[Season Four]] episode, "[[A Very Crappy Christmas]]" and ''[[South Park: The Stick of Truth]]''.

==Appearance==
Amber is a talking piece of excrement with a small purple dress and a red bow.

==Personality==
Amber is very innocent, and hopeful. She is notably just as "christmasy" as her father, Mr. Hankey.

==Prominence==
*"[[A Very Crappy Christmas]]" - First appearance. Follows [[Mr. Hankey]] around with her siblings.
*"[[I'm a Little Bit Country]]" - Seen in the crowd of people along with the rest of her family.

===''[[South Park: The Stick of Truth]]''===
She plays a minor role in Mr. Hankey's side quest, [[Dropping the Kids Off]]. Once [[The New Kid|the New Kid]] frees her she gives them a friend request and returns home.
