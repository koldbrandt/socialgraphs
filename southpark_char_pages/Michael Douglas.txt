'''Michael Kirk Douglas Jr''' is an American actor best known for his role in the [http://www.imdb.com/title/tt0443632/ ''The Sentinel'']. He first appears in "[[Sexual Healing]]", where he is treated for sex addiction at the [[Karne Institute for Sex Addiction]] and learned that his condition is caused by a [[Wizard Alien]].

==Appearance==
Michael Douglas has short, gray hair, various facial wrinkles, and thick, gray eyebrows. He wore a dull brown suit and  a light blue, "dress" shirt.
