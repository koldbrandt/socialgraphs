The '''Boy with Earmuffs''' is a recurring background character in ''[[South Park]]''. He is the only known fourth grader to wear earmuffs.

==Appearance==
He has short, light brown hair. He wears green earmuffs, a navy blue shirt with a green collar and dark gray pants. He also wears green gloves and black shoes.

==Trivia==
*He used to be known by fans as "Muffy".
*A lookalike of him without the earmuffs appears in several episodes, such as "[[Mystery of the Urinal Deuce]]".
