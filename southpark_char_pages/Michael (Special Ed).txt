'''Michael''' is male fourth grader who appears in several episodes with other handicapped children.

==Background==
Michael is seen in "[[Up the Down Steroid]]" at the Special Olympics, but appears prominently at [[Lake Tardicaca]] in "[[Crippled Summer]]". He is on the [[Red Team]] with [[Nathan]], [[Mimsy]], [[Francis (Special Ed)|Francis]], [[Chad Handler]], [[Reggie Burke]], [[Dennis Murray]], and [[Colette Francis]].

Michael has unspecified motor disabilities and an eye disorder as well as learning difficulties, along with many of the other special education class members.

==Appearance==
Michael wears a gray short-sleeved shirt, black pants, black shoes, and a red cap with a blue border. He has brown hair and is almost always seen with his eyes closed. Michael wears glasses with green lenses.

In "[[Crippled Summer]]" he wears the standard uniform of [[Lake Tardicaca]].
