The '''Rich Kid in Cherry Creek''' was one of [[The Boys|the boys']] tooth fairy theft victims. He appears in "[[The Tooth Fairy Tats 2000]]". He wakes up to discover [[Eric Cartman|Cartman]] as the Tooth Fairy.

==Appearance==
He has brown hair and a light-blue button shirt with a collar.
<gallery widths="160">
TheToothFairyTats2000040.png|''"I don't have any loose teeth, Miss Fairies but I have been a very good boy."''
</gallery>

==Personality==
He appears to be somewhat unintelligent, believing that Cartman and another unnamed boy were both the tooth fairy.
