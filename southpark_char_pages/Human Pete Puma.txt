'''Pete Puma''' is a character who appears in [[Season Fourteen]] episode, "[[Crippled Summer]]".

==Background==
He is a student at [[Lake Tardicaca|Tardicaca Camp]], which is a camp for children with disabilities. He was on the [[Blue Team]] with [[Jimmy]], [[Timmy]], [[Lenora]], [[Droopy Dawg]], and [[Elmer Fudd]]. He later appears at the end of the episode saying he never saw a child being raped by a shark, which was what had happened to [[Nathan]]. His disability makes him look and sound like the Pete Puma character

==Appearance==
He wears a blue long-sleeved shirt with the Lake Tardicaca logo and dark purple pants. He is almost always smiling and has a wide nose and a big mouth, his eyes are almost closed and he also has big hands. He has brown hair.
