'''Dave Bartles''' is a [[The 6th Graders|6th grade]] boy who attends [[South Park Elementary]]. Although appearing in the show many times, he was unnamed until the [[Season Twelve]] episode, "[[Britney's New Look]]", where he was referred to as "Dave". He was later revealed to be known as "Bartles" in ''[[South Park: The Fractured But Whole]]''.

==Appearance==
He has large ears and light-brown messy hair. He wears an orange and blue striped shirt and blue jeans. He can be seen riding a yellow bicycle.
