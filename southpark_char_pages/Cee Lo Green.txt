'''Cee Lo Green''', also known as '''CeeLo Green''', is an American singer-songwriter and rapper. He appears in the [[Season Sixteen]] episode, "[[Sarcastaball]]", where he sings the "[[I Love Sarcastaball]]" before the Denver Broncos and Oakland Raiders [[Sarcastaball (Sport)|sarcastaball]] game.

==Appearance==
Cee Lo Green is bald with a black goatee. He wears white shoes, a gray shirt, white sunglasses, dark gray pants, a red coat, and a golden chain.
