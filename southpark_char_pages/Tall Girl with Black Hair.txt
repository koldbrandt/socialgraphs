The '''Tall Girl with Black Hair''' is a [[South Park Elementary]] student who appears in "[[Stupid Spoiled Whore Video Playset]]" as a background character. She also appears in ''[[South Park: The Fractured But Whole]]'', where she is seen leaving school with the other kids.

==Appearance==
She has curly black hair and wears a purple striped jacket over a gray shirt and purple shorts.
