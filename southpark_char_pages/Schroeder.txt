'''Schroeder''', from the comic strip ''Peanuts'', appeared in the [[Season Four]] episodes, "[[Something You Can Do with Your Finger]]", "[[A Very Crappy Christmas]]", and "[[Probably]]". Although he often appeared whenever music auditions take place, he has yet to make another appearance.

==Appearance==
Schroeder wears white shoes, black pants, and a red and black striped shirt. He has thick blond hair. Schroeder is always seen playing the piano.
