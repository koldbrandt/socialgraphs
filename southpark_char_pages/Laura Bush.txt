'''Laura Lane Welch Bush '''is the former First Lady of the United States of America and the wife of [[George W. Bush|George Bush]]. She appears in the [[Season Five]] episode, "[[Super Best Friends]]".

== Background ==
She was seen with her husband, who was deciding whether to give [[David Blaine]] and his followers tax-exempt status or not.

==Appearance==
Laura Bush wears a blue business suit consisting of a white ascot, blue pants, and a blue coat. She has brown hair and noticeable signs of aging on her face.

==Trivia==
*The appearance is based on [[Carrie Quinn Dolan|Carrie Quinn Dolan's]] portrayal of Rove from [[Trey Parker]] and [[Matt Stone]]'s sitcom, ''That's My Bush''.
