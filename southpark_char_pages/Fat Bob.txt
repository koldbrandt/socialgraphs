'''Fat Bob''' is a relative of [[Eric Cartman]] in some way. His relation to the Cartman family is unknown. He made his first appearance in the episode "[[Merry Christmas Charlie Manson!]]", and appears again at [[Mabel Cartman]]'s funeral in "[[Cartmanland]]".

==Appearance==
He is overweight like most of his family and he wears a pale green t-shirt which reveals the top of his chest hair and a pair of brown trousers and black shoes.
