The '''Middle Park Cowboys''' is the name of the sports team at [[Middle Park Elementary]]. Their mascot is a cowboy. Their only appearance is in the [[Season One]] episode, "[[Big Gay Al's Big Gay Boat Ride]]". Notably, the team is shown to easily overwhelm the South Park Cows.

==Appearance==
Their uniform consisted of a gray, white-striped helmet with a revolver on it, a dark blue shirt, and gray pants.
