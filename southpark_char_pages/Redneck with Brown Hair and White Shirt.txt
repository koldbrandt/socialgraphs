The '''Redneck with Brown Hair and White Shirt''' is an unnamed character that is sometimes seen with other rednecks. He was first seen in the [[Season Eight]], episode "[[Goobacks]]".

==Appearance==
He has messy curly brown hair and a brown mustache. He wears a white jacket with green accents over a black collared T-shirt, and also wears dark blue trousers and black boots. He has a double chin and two forehead wrinkles and is overweight.
Sometimes he can be seen wearing a dark red jacket over a brown collar T-shirt.
