'''Charlie Brown''' is a main character on the comic strip ''Peanuts'' by [[Wikipedia:Charles M. Schulz|Charles M. Schulz]] who appeared in the [[Season Four]] episode "[[A Very Crappy Christmas]]", and in [[Season Eleven]] episodes "[[Imaginationland]]" and "[[Imaginationland, Episode II]]". 

==Appearance==
Charlie Brown in South Park is almost identical to his original design. He wears a yellow shirt with a black stripe, black shorts, white socks, and brown shoes. He's almost bald, with only a small curl of hair in front of his head. Unlike the [[Family Guy]] characters in the "[[Cartoon Wars Part I]]" and "[[Cartoon Wars Part II]]" episodes, Charlie Brown has the eyes and the same mouth shapes of a normal South Park character and has no black outline.
