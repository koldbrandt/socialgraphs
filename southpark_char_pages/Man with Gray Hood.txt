The '''man with Gray Hood''' is a [[City people|Citizen]] of [[South Park (Location)|South Park]] who appears in several current episodes as a background character, with his first appearance being in the [[Season Nine]] episode "[[Follow That Egg!]]". He is the alleged father of 6th grader [[Bartles]].

==Appearance==
He has dark brown hair and wears a gray hood over a cyan shirt and green pants.
