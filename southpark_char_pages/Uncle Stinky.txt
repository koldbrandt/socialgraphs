'''Uncle Stinky''' is the uncle of [[Eric Cartman]] who first appeared in the [[Season Two]] episode, "[[Merry Christmas Charlie Manson!]]". He is the husband of [[Lisa Cartman]] and the father of [[Elvin Cartman]] and [[Alexandra Cartman]].

==Background==
Uncle Stinky later appeared in "[[Cartmanland]]", where he now owns the house in Nebraska seen in "[[Merry Christmas Charlie Manson!]]" due to his mother, [[Mabel Cartman]], dying. Like most of the extended Cartman family, he hasn't made a recent appearance.

==Appearance==
He wears a dark green sweater with a matching pair of grey trousers and black shoes. He also has depicted facial hair consisting of a mustache that runs down the side of his nose and a small centered goatee. Like the rest of the men in the Cartman family he is obese.
