'''Brad The "PC Arizona State"''' is one of the [[PC Bros]] who first appear in the [[Season Nineteen]] episode, "[[Stunning and Brave]]".

==Appearance==
Brad wears a blue and black shirt with "24" on it, gray pants and red sneakers. Brad appears to have long, golden blond hair and is seen wearing a black cap with a purple border.

==Prominence==
*"[[Stunning and Brave]]" - Debut appearance; Seen at the [[PC Delta]].
*"[[Sponsored Content]]" - Seen at the [[PC Delta]]. He has been seen performing for handicapped children but when [[Jimmy]] is about to greet him, he is distracted by two women at the party.
