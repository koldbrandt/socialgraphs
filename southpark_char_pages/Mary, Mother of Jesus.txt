'''Mary, Mother of Jesus''' is a religious figure most known for giving birth to [[Jesus Christ]]. She appeared in "[[The Spirit of Christmas#Jesus vs. Frosty|Jesus vs. Frosty]]", as well as the [[Season Three]] episode, "[[Mr. Hankey's Christmas Classics]]" alongside [[Saint Joseph]] during the song, "[[O Holy Night]]". She is also mentioned in the song "[[The Most Offensive Song Ever]]".

==Appearance==
Mary has long brown hair and she wears a long white robe. She also wears a deep blue scarf and head shawl.
