The '''6th Grader Girl with Violet Shirt''' is a [[South Park Elementary]] student who only appears as a background character, she is usually seen on the [[South Park Elementary Playground|playground]] and in the [[South Park Elementary Cafeteria|cafeteria]].

==Appearance==
She has light brown hair with medium length and wears a red-violet shirt and blue jeans.
