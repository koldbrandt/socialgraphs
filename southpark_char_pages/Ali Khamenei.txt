'''Sayyid Ali Hosseini Khamenei''', often known as just '''Ali Khamenei''', is the incumbent Supreme Leader of [[Iran]] who appears in [[Season Seventeen]] episode "[[Ginger Cow]]".

==Background==
Upon discovery of a [[Ginger Cow (Animal)|red-haired cow]], the leaders of Islam, Christianity, and Judaism met to discuss the terms of an Armageddon-style war. Ali Khamenei suggested that if chemical weapons could not be used, then nuclear weapons should be ruled out as well. Later, he agrees on the use of non-ballistic nuclear weapons.

==Appearance==
Ali Khamenei wears black and grey robes. He has a thick beard and wears spectacles. He also wears a turban and is visibly aged.
